# How to Work with Me

I lead our PM team to deliver customer-obsessed products that scale massively and drive business impact. 
No fluff, no hype—just rigorous research, clear decisions, and relentless execution. 
My goal is to empower you to move fast while maintaining high standards. 
Let's build great things together.

## My Leadership Style
- **Research-first**: Every decision starts with data. I expect deep dives (e.g., competitive analysis, customer cohorts) before we commit.
- **Clarity absolutist**: Ambiguity is the enemy. I cut through noise—say what you mean, back it with facts, no casual chit-chat.
- **Concise commander**: Short meetings, precise language. I delegate ownership but hold accountability tight.
- **No-BS mentor**: Feedback is blunt and research-backed. Growth comes from facing hard truths, not pep talks.

## My Communication Style
- **Direct and concise**: I value clarity over politeness. If I say "this won't work," it's backed by data—ask for the analysis.
- **Async-first**: Slack for updates, email for decisions, Chime for syncs. Core hours: 9 AM–6 PM IST. Responses within 24 hours.
- **Data over opinions**: Metrics, experiments, or research only. "Gut feels" get shut down.

## Working Rhythms and Cadence
- **Weekly 1:1s**: 25 mins max—your updates, blockers, one data-backed ask. I'll share priorities.
- **Team syncs**: Tuesdays 10 AM IST (25 mins). Agenda required, no tangents.
- **Reviews**: Quarterly roadmaps with research packs; PR/FAQs for launches.
- **Off-hours**: Disconnected after 7 PM IST/weekends. Urgent? On-call only.

## Expectations from You
- **Ownership**: Run your project like it's yours. Propose research-supported solutions.
- **Customer obsession**: Quantify needs (e.g., "20% of Segment X churns here—hypothesis?").
- **Bias for action**: Ship MVPs, measure, iterate. Analysis paralysis fails.
- **Dive deep**: Root-causes with data trails.

## What Success Looks Like
| Role | Key Outcomes I Expect |
|------|-----------------------|
| PMs | Hit OKRs, 4+ launches/quarter, 10% churn reduction via experiments. |
| Eng/Design | On-time, zero P0 bugs, research-validated designs. |
| Stakeholders | Data dashboards, aligned roadmaps. |

## pet peeves
- Loose talk or unbacked opinions.
- Scope creep without tradeoff analysis.
- Meetings without crisp agendas/outcomes.

## Quick Tips for Best Results
1. Lead with: "Problem + data + proposed fix + research."
2. Use my PRD template (Slack me).
3. Wins? Post metrics in #wins—no anecdotes.
