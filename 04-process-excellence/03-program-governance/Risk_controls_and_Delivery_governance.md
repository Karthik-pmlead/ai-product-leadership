# 🛡️ Risk Controls + Delivery Governance

> Predictable multi-team execution through structured governance, risk management, and delivery controls.

Engineering and AI product organizations require operational governance mechanisms that reduce execution volatility, improve delivery visibility, and maintain alignment across teams, dependencies, and strategic priorities.

---

# 📊 Core Outcomes

- Predictable cross-team delivery
- Reduced scope volatility
- Faster escalation management
- Improved executive visibility
- Standardized execution governance
- Lower operational risk

---

# ⚙️ Governance Framework

| Governance Area | Control Mechanism | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Risk Management | RAID register | Early issue visibility |
| Scope Governance | Intake + approval workflows | Reduced scope creep |
| Dependency Tracking | Cross-functional syncs | Fewer delivery blockers |
| KPI Monitoring | Delivery dashboards | Real-time visibility |
| Escalation Management | Defined ownership paths | Faster resolution |

---

# 🚀 Core Governance Components

## 1. RAID Management

Track:
- Risks
- Assumptions
- Issues
- Dependencies

### Governance Rules
- Every risk assigned an owner
- Severity scoring mandatory
- Mitigation plans required
- Weekly review cadence enforced

---

## 2. Scope & Intake Governance

### Intake Requirements
- Business objective
- OKR alignment
- Resource estimate
- Risk assessment
- Dependency review

### Scope Control Rules
- All scope changes require approval
- High-risk initiatives escalated to leadership
- Changes linked to measurable business impact

---

## 3. Dependency Management

| Dependency Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Engineering | Shared infrastructure |
| Data | Pipeline readiness |
| Vendor | External integrations |
| Product | Requirement dependencies |

### Coordination Mechanisms
- Weekly dependency reviews
- Shared delivery dashboards
- Escalation workflows
- Cross-team planning syncs
