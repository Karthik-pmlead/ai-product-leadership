# 🎯 OKR Cascade Template

> Structured alignment framework connecting company strategy → portfolio priorities → team execution → sprint delivery.

---

# 📊 Objective

Ensure every engineering, product, and operational initiative directly supports measurable business outcomes.

---

# OKR Cascade Structure

```text
Company OKRs
      ↓
Portfolio / Business Unit OKRs
      ↓
Team OKRs
      ↓
Epics & Sprint Tasks
```

---

# 1. Company OKRs (Annual)

## Strategic Objective

| Objective | Owner | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Improve AI product adoption | CEO | Annual |
| Increase platform reliability | CTO | Annual |

---

## Key Results

| KR | Target | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| KR1 | +25% active users | MAU |
| KR2 | <1% production failure rate | Reliability |
| KR3 | +15% enterprise expansion | Revenue |

# 📈 OKR Planning Rules

| Rule | Description |
|---|---|
| Max Company OKRs | 3–5 annually |
| Bottom-Up Contribution | 30–40% team-driven |
| KR Ownership | Single accountable owner |
| Sprint Traceability | Every epic linked to KR |
| Review Cadence | Quarterly |

---

# 📏 Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| OKR Achievement Rate | >80% |
| Sprint Predictability | >90% |
| Delivery Alignment | 100% KR-linked work |
| Scope Change Rate | <5% |

---

# 🔄 Review Cadence

| Cadence | Activity |
|---|---|
| Weekly | Sprint progress review |
| Monthly | Portfolio alignment review |
| Quarterly | OKR retrospective |
| Annual | Strategic reset |

---

# ⚠️ Common Failure Modes

| Failure Pattern | Root Cause |
|---|---|
| Too many OKRs | Lack of prioritization |
| Weak KR measurement | Non-quantifiable outcomes |
| Misaligned sprint work | Missing KR mapping |
| Scope creep | Uncontrolled intake process |
