# Productivity Products Metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Throughput | Units of work completed in a period. | Core output metric. | Great for teams and systems. |
| Velocity | Work completed per sprint or time box. | Delivery speed. | Common in agile teams. |
| Cycle Time | Time from start to completion of work. | Efficiency measure. | Lower is better. |
| Lead Time | Time from request to delivery. | End-to-end responsiveness. | Includes waiting time. |
| Completion Rate | Percentage of tasks completed. | Execution reliability. | Simple productivity metric. |
| Task Completion Time | Time to finish a task. | Individual or workflow efficiency. | Useful in tools and automation. |
| First Response Time | Time to first response in a workflow. | Responsiveness. | Good for support and collaboration tools. |
| Resolution Time | Time to complete a request or ticket. | Operational speed. | Important in service workflows. |
| Backlog Size | Number of unfinished items waiting. | Queue health. | High backlog can indicate bottlenecks. |
| WIP | Active items currently being worked on. | Flow efficiency. | Helps control overload. |
| Utilization Rate | Share of time spent on productive work. | Resource efficiency. | Can be misleading if too high. |
| Idle Time | Time not spent on productive work. | Workflow waste. | Useful in operations. |
| Rework Rate | Work needing correction or repeat effort. | Quality and efficiency. | Important in product and ops. |
| Error Rate | Mistakes per unit of work. | Quality signal. | Can increase productivity costs. |
| Defect Density | Defects per unit of output. | Engineering quality. | Common in software teams. |
| SLA Compliance Rate | Tasks completed within SLA. | Service performance. | Strong for operations teams. |
| On-Time Delivery Rate | Work delivered on schedule. | Planning reliability. | Works in many industries. |
| Process Adherence Rate | Share of tasks following defined process. | Operational consistency. | Useful in enterprise workflows. |
| Handoff Time | Time lost between steps or teams. | Bottleneck detection. | Important for cross-functional work. |
| Automation Rate | Percentage of work automated. | Productivity leverage. | Good for AI/workflow products. |
| Self-Service Rate | Users resolving tasks without human help. | Efficiency and scalability. | Strong for support and enterprise tools. |
| Search-to-Action Rate | Searches that lead to completed work. | Tool usefulness. | Helpful in knowledge tools. |
| Knowledge Reuse Rate | Reuse of templates, docs, or assets. | Productivity multiplier. | Great for internal tools. |
| Meeting Load | Time spent in meetings. | Productivity drain. | Useful in knowledge work. |
| Focus Time | Uninterrupted work time. | Deep work productivity. | Good for productivity platforms. |
| Decision Turnaround Time | Time to make a decision. | Workflow speed. | Important in approvals and operations. |
| Escalation Rate | Number of issues requiring escalation. | Workflow friction. | Useful in support and enterprise operations. |
