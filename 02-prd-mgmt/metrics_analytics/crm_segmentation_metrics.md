# CRM and Segmentation Metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| RFM Score | Recency, Frequency, Monetary value. | Customer segmentation. | Great for lifecycle analysis. |
| Lead Score | Likelihood of conversion. | Sales prioritization. | Common in CRM. |
| MQL Rate | Marketing qualified leads. | Funnel quality. | Bridge between marketing and sales. |
| SQL Rate | Sales qualified leads. | Sales readiness. | Important in B2B. |
| Opportunity Conversion Rate | Opportunities that close. | Revenue effectiveness. | Strong CRM metric. |
| Pipeline Value | Total value of open deals. | Sales forecasting. | Core CRM view. |
| Win Rate | Closed-won deals divided by opportunities. | Sales efficiency. | Important for forecasting. |
| Average Deal Size | Average value per closed deal. | Revenue quality. | Useful in enterprise sales. |
| Sales Cycle Length | Time to close a deal. | Sales speed. | Lower is better. |
| Expansion Revenue | Revenue from existing customers. | Growth from account management. | Critical in SaaS. |
| Customer Churn Rate | Customers lost over time. | Retention measure. | Works in subscriptions and CRM. |
| Renewal Rate | Customers renewing contracts. | Account health. | Strong for B2B and SaaS. |
| Adoption Rate | Share of users actively using the product. | CRM value realization. | Important for enterprise software. |
| Feature Adoption | Usage of specific features by users or accounts. | Product relevance. | Useful in CRM and SaaS. |
| Account Health Score | Combined view of engagement and risk. | Customer success prioritization. | Common in CRM systems. |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Value of customer over time. | Profitability and prioritization. | Often paired with CAC. |
| Segmentation Accuracy | Quality of customer grouping. | Targeting effectiveness. | Useful in marketing CRM. |
| Campaign Response Rate | Responses to CRM campaigns. | Outreach effectiveness. | Important for lifecycle marketing. |
| NPS by Segment | Loyalty split across customer groups. | Identifies high-value cohorts. | Better than blended NPS alone. |
