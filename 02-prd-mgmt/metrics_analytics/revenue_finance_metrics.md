# Revenue and Finance Metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| MRR | Monthly recurring revenue. | Subscription revenue health. | SaaS core metric. |
| ARR | Annual recurring revenue. | Annualized revenue scale. | SaaS planning. |
| ARPU | Revenue per user. | Monetization efficiency. | Can be per customer too. |
| Gross Margin | Revenue after direct costs. | Profitability. | Important in all business models. |
| Net Revenue Retention | Revenue retained from existing customers. | Expansion and churn balance. | Core SaaS metric. |
| Gross Revenue Retention | Revenue retained before expansion. | Churn quality. | Used in B2B. |
| LTV / CLV | Customer value over lifecycle. | Profitability. | Compare against CAC. |
| AOV | Average order value. | Transaction value. | E-commerce and retail. |
| Margin per Order | Profit per order. | Unit economics. | Strong commerce metric. |
| Contribution Margin | Revenue after variable costs. | Business unit health. | Important in marketplaces. |
| Burn Rate | Cash spent per period. | Startup runway. | Finance metric. |
| Runway | Time before cash runs out. | Survival planning. | Important for startups. |
| EBITDA | Operational profitability. | Financial performance. | Common in larger businesses. |
| Revenue Growth Rate | Period-over-period growth. | Momentum. | Important across models. |
| Net Revenue | Revenue after deductions. | True earned revenue. | Better than gross revenue alone. |
