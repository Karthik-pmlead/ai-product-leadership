# Product Quality and Support Metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CSAT | Satisfaction after interaction. | Support and transaction quality. | Good after specific events. |
| NPS | Loyalty and advocacy. | Brand strength. | Best as a directional metric. |
| CES | Customer effort score. | Ease of use. | Strong for support and workflows. |
| First Contact Resolution | Issues solved on first contact. | Support efficiency. | Important for service products. |
| Average Handle Time | Time spent per support interaction. | Support productivity. | Use carefully with quality metrics. |
| Ticket Volume | Number of support cases. | Friction detection. | Spikes can signal issues. |
| Ticket Backlog | Open unresolved tickets. | Service health. | Operational risk metric. |
| Escalation Rate | Tickets needing escalation. | Complexity and support quality. | Use with resolution time. |
| Bug Rate | Bugs per release or unit. | Product quality. | Strong engineering metric. |
| Crash Rate | App crashes per session or user. | Mobile stability. | Important for consumer apps. |
| Uptime | Service availability. | Reliability. | Core infrastructure metric. |
| Latency | Response time of systems. | Experience quality. | Critical for performance-sensitive products. |
