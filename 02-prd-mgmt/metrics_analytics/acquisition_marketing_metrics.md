# Acquisition and Marketing Metrics

## Overview

Acquisition and marketing metrics measure how efficiently a business creates awareness, traffic, leads, and customers. They are most useful when compared across channels, campaigns, and funnels [web:153][web:255][web:258].

## Core metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPC | Cost per click. | Paid traffic efficiency. | Common top-funnel metric [web:153][web:255][web:258]. |
| CPM | Cost per thousand impressions. | Awareness buying efficiency. | Useful for reach campaigns [web:153][web:255][web:258]. |
| CTR | Click-through rate. | Creative and targeting quality. | Measures ad relevance [web:153][web:255][web:258]. |
| CPA | Cost per acquisition or action. | Conversion efficiency. | One of the most important paid metrics [web:153][web:255][web:258]. |
| ROAS | Revenue generated per unit of ad spend. | Marketing return. | Core paid media metric [web:153][web:255][web:258]. |
| CVR | Conversion rate. | Funnel effectiveness. | Shows how many clicks become conversions [web:153][web:258]. |
| Lead Conversion Rate | Percentage of leads that become customers. | Lead quality. | Important in B2B and mixed funnels. |
| Impression Share | Share of eligible impressions won. | Competitive reach. | Useful in search and paid media. |
| Organic Acquisition Rate | Users acquired organically. | Channel mix health. | Useful for efficiency. |
| Bounce Rate | Percentage of users who leave after one page/session. | Landing-page quality. | Indicates mismatch or weak relevance. |
| CAC | Cost to acquire a customer. | Growth efficiency. | Connects marketing to business economics. |
| MER | Marketing efficiency ratio. | Broad marketing performance. | Revenue divided by total marketing spend [web:153][web:258]. |

## Funnel view

| Funnel stage | Relevant metrics |
|---|---|
| Awareness | CPM, Impressions, Reach, Impression Share |
| Consideration | CTR, Bounce Rate, Session Duration |
| Conversion | CVR, CPA, Lead Conversion Rate |
| Revenue | ROAS, AOV, CAC |
| Efficiency | MER, CAC Payback Period |

## Use cases

| Use case | Most relevant metrics |
|---|---|
| Paid acquisition | CPC, CPM, CTR, CPA, ROAS |
| Lead generation | CTR, Lead Conversion Rate, CPA, CAC |
| Brand awareness | CPM, Reach, Impressions, Viewability |
| E-commerce growth | CTR, CVR, ROAS, AOV, CAC |

## Practical tips

| Tip | Why it helps |
|---|---|
| Compare metrics by channel | Search, social, display, and email behave differently. |
| Pair top-funnel and bottom-funnel metrics | A campaign can have cheap clicks but poor conversions. |
| Use revenue metrics, not only traffic metrics | Traffic alone does not prove business value. |
| Track blended and channel-specific results | Helps avoid over-optimizing one channel. |

## Interview use

In interviews, explain acquisition metrics as the funnel from reach to click to conversion to revenue. Then show how CPC, CTR, CPA, and ROAS help evaluate marketing efficiency.
