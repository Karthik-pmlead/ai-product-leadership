# Acquisition and Marketing Metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Why it matters | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPC | Cost per click. | Paid traffic efficiency. | Common top-funnel metric. |
| CPM | Cost per thousand impressions. | Awareness buying efficiency. | Strong for media spend. |
| CPA | Cost per acquisition or action. | Conversion efficiency. | Also cost per conversion. |
| ROAS | Revenue per ad spend. | Marketing return. | Paid media core metric. |
| Conversion Rate | Ad or landing-page conversion rate. | Acquisition effectiveness. | Use by funnel stage. |
| Lead Conversion Rate | Leads converted into customers. | Funnel quality. | Especially B2B. |
| CTR | Click-through rate. | Creative and targeting quality. | Use with impressions. |
| Bounce Rate | Landing-page mismatch. | Traffic quality. | Important in paid acquisition. |
| Organic Acquisition Rate | Users acquired organically. | Channel mix health. | Useful for efficiency. |
| CAC Payback Period | Time to recover CAC. | Growth sustainability. | Strong SaaS metric. |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | Value vs acquisition cost. | Business viability. | One of the most important ratios. |
