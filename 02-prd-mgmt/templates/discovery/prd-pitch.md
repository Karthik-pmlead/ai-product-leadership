# PRD Pitch

## Overview

This document is a short pitch used to get early alignment before writing the full PRD.

## Pitch summary

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Product idea | Guided onboarding and lifecycle nudges |
| Owner | Product Manager |
| Date | 2026-05-20 |

## Problem

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Current pain | New users do not reach first value quickly enough. |
| Business impact | Activation and retention are below target. |

## Proposed solution

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Solution | Add onboarding checklist, tooltips, and lifecycle emails. |
| Why it works | It helps users understand what to do next and brings them back if they stall. |

## Expected impact

| Metric | Expected change |
|---|---|
| Activation rate | +18 points |
| D7 retention | +7 points |
| Support tickets | -15% |

## Scope

| In scope | Out of scope |
|---|---|
| Checklist, tooltips, nudges, event tracking | Enterprise onboarding, full redesign, new billing flow |

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Too many changes at once | Ship in phases. |
| Nudges may annoy users | Use frequency controls. |

## Ask

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Decision needed | Approve writing the full PRD and discovery plan. |

## Notes

- Keep the pitch short.
- Focus on the problem, solution, and expected value.
