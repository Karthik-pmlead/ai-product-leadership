# One-Pager

## Overview

This document gives a fast summary of the idea, audience, value, and impact.

## Idea summary

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Idea | Guided onboarding for new users |
| Audience | New self-serve users |
| Owner | Product Manager |

## Why this matters

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Pain | Users do not understand the next best step. |
| Impact | Low activation and weak retention. |
| Opportunity | Reduce friction and help users get value sooner. |

## What we will do

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Add onboarding checklist | Show the next key setup steps. |
| Add tooltips | Explain important UI elements. |
| Add lifecycle nudges | Bring users back if they stall. |

## Expected impact

| Metric | Expected change |
|---|---|
| Activation rate | Increase from 32% to 50% |
| D7 retention | Increase from 18% to 25% |
| Support tickets | Decrease by 15% |

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Users ignore the checklist | Keep it short and contextual. |
| Too many messages | Add frequency limits. |
