# Learning Templates

Reusable templates for postmortems, retrospectives, and learning from incidents or launches.

## Navigation

| Template | Purpose | File |
|---|---|---|
| Postmortem | Document what happened, why it happened, and what will be done next. | [postmortem.md](./postmortem.md) |
| Retro | Capture what worked, what didn’t, and what to improve. | [retro.md](./retro.md) |

## What’s inside

| File | Best for |
|---|---|
| `postmortem.md` | Incident review, root cause analysis, and follow-up actions. |
| `retro.md` | Team reflection, process improvement, and continuous learning. |

## Suggested use order

1. Use `postmortem.md` after a major incident, launch issue, or failure.
2. Use `retro.md` after a release, sprint, project, or event to improve future work.

## Notes

- Keep the tone blameless and factual.
- Focus on learning and prevention.
- Track action items until they are closed.
