# templates

A practical library of reusable product, strategy, launch, and enablement templates.

Use these templates to move from idea to approval, planning to launch, and rollout to learning.

## How to use this repo

1. Start with strategy and approval templates when you need buy-in.
2. Use discovery templates to define the problem and validate the opportunity.
3. Use planning templates to align execution and manage risk.
4. Use launch templates to communicate clearly at release time.
5. Use enablement templates to help users and internal teams adopt the product.
6. Use learning templates to capture feedback and improve future work.

## Naming convention

- Use lowercase file names.
- Use hyphens between words.
- Prefix folders with numbers to preserve order.
- Example: `01-strategy-approval/business-case.md`, `04-launch/release-notes.md`.

## Notes

- Some templates are intentionally lightweight, while others are decision-heavy.
- A PRD defines what and why.
- A business case explains why the investment should happen.
- A press release tells the story externally.
- A release note summarizes what changed.
- A postmortem captures learning after launch or incident.

## Maintenance

- Update templates as your process matures.
- Keep each template short, practical, and reusable.
- Add examples where people commonly struggle.
- Review templates after major launches or planning cycles.
