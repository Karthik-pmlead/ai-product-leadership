## Headline

ExampleCo Launches Guided Onboarding to Help New Users Reach Value Faster

## Dateline

Bengaluru, India — June 12, 2026

## Lead paragraph

ExampleCo today announced Guided Onboarding, a new product experience designed to help new users reach value faster. The feature introduces a checklist, contextual help, and lifecycle nudges to improve activation and retention.

## Body

Guided Onboarding helps users understand the next best step as soon as they sign up. Instead of exploring the product without direction, users are guided through the key actions that lead to first value.

The launch is intended for self-serve users who need a simpler path to setup and for teams that want to reduce repetitive support questions. ExampleCo expects the feature to improve activation and shorten the time it takes users to see value.

## Quote

“Users should not have to guess how to get started,” said Maya Rao, VP of Product at ExampleCo. “Guided Onboarding helps people reach value quickly while giving our team a better foundation for long-term retention.”
