# Risk Register

## Overview

This document tracks known risks, their likelihood, impact, mitigation, and owners. It helps the team identify problems early and manage them before they affect delivery or launch.

## Project details

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Project | Guided onboarding and lifecycle nudges |
| Owner | Product Manager |
| Date | 2026-05-20 |
| Status | Draft |

## Risk log

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Severity | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Users ignore onboarding checklist | High | High | High | Keep checklist short and contextual. | Product | Open |
| Lifecycle emails feel spammy | Medium | High | High | Add frequency caps and segmentation. | Marketing | Open |
| Event tracking is incomplete | Medium | High | High | Validate tracking before launch. | Data | Open |
| Engineering delivery slips | Medium | Medium | Medium | Freeze scope and review weekly. | Engineering | Open |
| Support team is not ready | Low | Medium | Medium | Share a support guide and FAQs before launch. | Support | Open |

## Risk categories

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Product | Scope creep, poor UX, unclear value proposition |
| Delivery | Engineering delays, dependency issues, test failures |
| Data | Missing instrumentation, inaccurate reporting |
| Launch | Messaging mismatch, timing issues, low adoption |
| Support | Incomplete documentation, untrained agents |

## Review cadence

| Cadence | Action |
|---|---|
| Weekly | Review open risks and update mitigation progress. |
| Before launch | Confirm high-severity risks are addressed. |
| After launch | Close resolved risks and add new issues if needed. |

## Notes

- Keep risks specific and actionable.
- Assign one owner per risk.
- Update the register as soon as new risks appear.
