# Product Proposal

## Overview

This document frames an early product idea and explains why it is worth exploring.

## Proposal summary

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Product / feature | Guided onboarding flow |
| Owner | Product Manager |
| Date | 2026-05-20 |
| Status | Draft |

## Problem

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| What is happening | New users sign up but fail to reach first value. |
| Who is affected | Self-serve users and the support team. |
| Why it matters | Low activation reduces retention and monetization. |

## Proposed idea

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Idea | Add guided onboarding with checklists and tooltips. |
| Why now | Activation is below target and support questions are increasing. |
| Expected outcome | More users reach first success faster. |

## Supporting evidence

| Evidence | Detail |
|---|---|
| Funnel drop-off | 68% of users do not finish setup. |
| Support data | Top ticket theme is “how do I get started?” |
| Competitive signal | Competitors offer guided setup and onboarding checklists. |

## Non-goals

| Non-goal | Why not |
|---|---|
| Full product redesign | Too large for initial exploration. |
| Enterprise onboarding | Not the primary target segment. |

## Success metrics

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|---|---:|---:|
| Activation rate | 32% | 50% |
| D7 retention | 18% | 25% |
| Setup completion rate | 24% | 45% |
