# ⚔️ Offensive + Defensive Strategy Framework

## 📌 What is it?

The **Offensive + Defensive Strategy** framework helps PMs and businesses decide:

> How to grow aggressively (offensive) and how to protect existing market position (defensive)

It is widely used in:
- Product strategy
- Competitive analysis
- Market expansion planning

---

## 🧠 Core Idea

- 🟢 **Offensive Strategy → Grow & capture market**
- 🔵 **Defensive Strategy → Protect & retain market**

👉 Strong companies do BOTH simultaneously.

---

## ⏰ When to Use It

Use this framework when:

- Entering a **competitive market**
- Planning **growth strategy**
- Facing **strong competitors**
- Trying to **increase market share**
- Protecting **existing users/revenue**
- Launching new **product lines or features**

👉 Best for: **strategy, growth, competition planning**

---

## 📍 Where to Use It

- Product strategy docs  
- Competitive analysis  
- GTM (Go-To-Market) planning  
- Roadmap prioritization  
- Business expansion strategy  

---

## ⚙️ How to Use It

### Step 1: Analyze your market position
- Are you leader or challenger?
- Are users loyal or switching easily?

### Step 2: Identify offensive moves
- How do we grow faster than competitors?

### Step 3: Identify defensive moves
- How do we prevent users from leaving?

### Step 4: Balance both strategies
- Growth without retention = waste  
- Retention without growth = stagnation  

---

## 🟢 Offensive Strategy (Grow Market Share)

### 🎯 Goal:
Acquire new users and expand usage

### 🔥 Tactics:

- Aggressive pricing / discounts
- New feature launches
- Market expansion (new cities/countries)
- Viral loops / referrals
- Product-led growth
- Partnerships & bundling

---

## 🔵 Defensive Strategy (Protect Market Share)

### 🎯 Goal:
Retain users and reduce churn

### 🔥 Tactics:

- Loyalty programs
- Better UX / reliability
- Switching cost creation
- Personalization
- Strong customer support
- Ecosystem lock-in (subscriptions, bundles)

---

## 🍔 Example (Food Delivery App)

| Strategy Type | Example |
|--------------|--------|
| 🟢 Offensive | Launch ₹0 delivery campaign to attract new users |
| 🟢 Offensive | Expand to new tier-2 cities |
| 🟢 Offensive | Referral bonus program |
| 🔵 Defensive | Improve delivery ETA accuracy |
| 🔵 Defensive | Loyalty rewards for frequent users |
| 🔵 Defensive | Faster refunds + better support |

---

## 🧠 Simple Mental Model

- 🟢 Offensive → “How do we grow faster?”
- 🔵 Defensive → “How do we stop losing users?”

---

## 🔥 PM Insight

> Winning companies don’t just acquire users — they also build walls to retain them.

- Offensive = **growth engine**
- Defensive = **retention engine**

👉 Sustainable products balance both.
