# Product Vision Board

## Overview

A Product Vision Board is a strategic framework used to align teams around:
- product vision
- target customers
- business goals
- value proposition

Popularized by:
- Roman Pichler

---

# Core Sections

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| Vision | Future product impact |
| Target Group | Intended users/customers |
| Needs | Problems to solve |
| Product | Proposed solution |
| Business Goals | Desired business outcomes |

---

# Example

| Section | Example |
|---|---|
| Vision | Simplify team collaboration |
| Target Group | Remote teams |
| Needs | Async communication |
| Product | AI productivity workspace |
| Goal | Increase retention |

---

# Best Used When

- Product inception
- Roadmap alignment
- Stakeholder communication
- Product strategy workshops

---

# Advantages

- Creates shared alignment
- Clarifies product direction
- Easy communication artifact

---

# Limitations

- High-level abstraction
- Requires regular updates
- Doesn't replace roadmap planning

---

# Best Practices

- Keep concise
- Focus on outcomes
- Align with customer needs
- Review periodically
