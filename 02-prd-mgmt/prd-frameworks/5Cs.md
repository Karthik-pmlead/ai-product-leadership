# 🧭 5Cs Framework (Marketing & Strategy)

## 📌 What is it?

The **5Cs Framework** is a strategic analysis tool used to understand the **business environment** before making product, marketing, or business decisions.

It helps PMs and strategists evaluate both **internal and external factors**.

---

## 🧠 5Cs Meaning

- 🏢 Company  
- 👥 Customers  
- 🤝 Competitors  
- 🧭 Collaborators  
- 🌍 Context  

---

## ⏰ When to Use 5Cs

Use 5Cs when:

- Doing **market or product strategy**
- Planning a **GTM (Go-To-Market) strategy**
- Entering a **new market**
- Analyzing **business environment**
- Preparing **product positioning**

👉 Best for: **strategy + market understanding**

---

## 📍 Where to Use It

- Product strategy docs  
- Market research  
- Business planning  
- Competitive analysis  
- Startup validation  

---

## ⚙️ 5Cs Breakdown

| C | Meaning | What to Analyze |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 🏢 Company | Internal strengths & capabilities | Product, team, resources, vision |
| 👥 Customers | Target users & needs | Segments, behavior, pain points |
| 🤝 Competitors | Market players | Direct & indirect competitors |
| 🧭 Collaborators | Partners & ecosystem | Suppliers, platforms, alliances |
| 🌍 Context | External environment | Economy, regulation, trends |

---

## 🍔 Example (Food Delivery App)

| C | Example |
|---|--------|
| Company | Strong logistics + delivery network |
| Customers | Urban users who want fast food delivery |
| Competitors | Swiggy, Zomato, Uber Eats |
| Collaborators | Restaurants, payment gateways |
| Context | Rising smartphone usage + UPI payments |

---

## 🧠 Simple Mental Model

- 🏢 What do we have?
- 👥 Who are we serving?
- 🤝 Who are we fighting?
- 🧭 Who can help us?
- 🌍 What external forces matter?

---

## 🚀 PM Insight

> 5Cs help you zoom out before building anything.

It ensures:
- Better strategic decisions  
- Stronger market understanding  
- Reduced blind spots
