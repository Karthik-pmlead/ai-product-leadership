# Cost of Delay

## Overview

Cost of Delay (CoD) measures the economic impact of delaying a feature, initiative, or project.

It helps teams prioritize work that loses value over time.

---

# Core Idea

Delays reduce value.

The longer something is postponed:
- revenue may be lost
- opportunities disappear
- risks increase
- customers churn

# Components

| Component | Description |
|---|---|
| Business Value | Revenue or strategic importance |
| Time Criticality | Urgency of delivery |
| Risk Reduction | Value from reducing uncertainty |

---

# Best Used When

- Portfolio prioritization
- Enterprise planning
- SAFe environments
- Time-sensitive projects
- Revenue-impacting initiatives

---

# Advantages

- Encourages economic thinking
- Highlights urgency
- Improves prioritization quality
- Useful at scale

---

# Limitations

- Hard to estimate precisely
- Requires stakeholder alignment
- Can become overly financial

---

# Example

| Initiative | Delay Impact |
|---|---|
| Compliance feature | Legal penalties |
| Holiday launch | Lost seasonal revenue |
| Security patch | Increased risk exposure |
