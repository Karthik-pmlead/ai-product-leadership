# Amazon Working Backwards

## Overview

Working Backwards is a product development approach popularized by:
- Amazon

The process starts with:
> the customer outcome first

before building the solution.

---

# Core Principle

Instead of asking:
> “What can we build?”

Ask:
> “What customer problem are we solving?”

---

# Key Artifacts

| Artifact | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Press Release | Describe future customer value |
| FAQ | Address expected questions |
| Customer Benefits | Clarify user impact |

---

# Process Flow

```text
Customer Problem
        ↓
Future Press Release
        ↓
FAQ Creation
        ↓
Refinement
        ↓
Build Product
```

---

# Press Release Structure

Typical sections:
- headline
- customer problem
- solution
- customer quote
- executive quote
- benefits

---

# FAQ Categories

| FAQ Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Customer FAQ | How does this help users? |
| Internal FAQ | What are implementation challenges? |

---

# Best Used When

- Product discovery
- Innovation initiatives
- Strategic product planning
- New product proposals

---

# Advantages

- Customer-centric thinking
- Forces clarity before execution
- Reduces solution-first bias

---

# Limitations

- Requires strong writing culture
- Hard for incremental improvements

---

# Best Practices

- Focus on customer outcomes
- Write clearly and concisely
- Validate assumptions early
