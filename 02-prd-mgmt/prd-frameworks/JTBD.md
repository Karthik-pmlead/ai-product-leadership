# 🧠 Jobs To Be Done (JTBD) Framework

## 📌 What is it?

**JTBD (Jobs To Be Done)** is a product discovery framework that focuses on:

> “What job is the user trying to get done?”

Instead of focusing on features or user demographics, it focuses on the **underlying motivation and outcome**.

---

## 🧠 Core Idea

People don’t buy products — they **“hire” them to get a job done**.

---

## ⏰ When to Use JTBD

Use JTBD when:

- You want to understand **real user motivation**
- You are building a **new product or feature**
- You are improving **product-market fit**
- You are analyzing **why users switch products**
- You want to go beyond **personas and demographics**

👉 Best for: **product discovery + user insight**

---

## 📍 Where to Use It

- Product discovery phase  
- UX research & interviews  
- Feature prioritization  
- Market research  
- Product strategy
