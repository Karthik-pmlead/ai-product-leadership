# SPACE Framework

## Overview

SPACE is a developer productivity framework created by:
- GitHub
- Microsoft Research
- University research collaborators

It argues that:
> developer productivity cannot be measured by a single metric.

SPACE balances:
- performance
- collaboration
- satisfaction
- efficiency

---

# SPACE Dimensions

| Letter | Meaning |
|---|---|
| S | Satisfaction & Well-being |
| P | Performance |
| A | Activity |
| C | Communication & Collaboration |
| E | Efficiency & Flow |

---

# Dimension Breakdown

## Satisfaction & Well-being

Measures:
- developer happiness
- burnout
- morale

Examples:
- surveys
- retention
- engagement

---

## Performance

Measures:
- business outcomes
- engineering impact

Examples:
- reliability
- customer outcomes
- quality

---

## Activity

Measures:
- visible work output

Examples:
- commits
- PRs
- deployments

NOTE:
Activity alone ≠ productivity.

---

## Communication & Collaboration

Measures:
- teamwork effectiveness

Examples:
- review participation
- knowledge sharing
- cross-team alignment

---

## Efficiency & Flow

Measures:
- ability to work uninterrupted

Examples:
- cycle time
- context switching
- build times

---

# Core Principle

Healthy engineering organizations optimize:
- systems
not individual output alone.

---

# Best Used When

- Engineering leadership
- Developer productivity analysis
- Organizational health measurement
- Platform engineering

---

# Advantages

- Holistic productivity view
- Reduces toxic metric obsession
- Encourages sustainable engineering culture

---

# Limitations

- Difficult to instrument fully
- Requires qualitative + quantitative inputs

---

# Example

## Poor Metric Usage

Tracking:

SPACE instead evaluates:
- quality
