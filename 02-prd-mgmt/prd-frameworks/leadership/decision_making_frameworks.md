# Decision-Making Frameworks

## Overview

Decision-making frameworks provide structured approaches for:
- evaluating options
- handling uncertainty
- prioritizing tradeoffs
- making strategic decisions

They improve:
- consistency
- speed
- clarity
- accountability

---

# Why Decision Frameworks Matter

Without structured decision-making:
- bias increases
- alignment decreases
- execution slows
- tradeoffs become unclear

| Trap | Description |
|---|---|

| Confirmation Bias | Seeking validating evidence |
| HiPPO Decisions | Highest-paid opinion dominates |
