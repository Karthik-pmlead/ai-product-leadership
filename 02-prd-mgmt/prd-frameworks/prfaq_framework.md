# PRFAQ Framework

## Overview

PRFAQ stands for:
- Press Release
- Frequently Asked Questions

Popularized by:
- Amazon

It is a product strategy and proposal framework used to:
- clarify product vision
- validate customer value
- align stakeholders

---

# Core Idea

Teams write the:
> future product announcement first

before building anything.

This forces:
- customer-centric thinking
- strategic clarity

---

# PRFAQ Structure

## Press Release

Describes:
- customer problem
- solution
- benefits
- differentiation

Written as if the product already launched.

---

## FAQ

Answers:
- customer questions
- operational concerns
- technical risks
- business tradeoffs

---

# Example Sections

| Section | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Headline | Product announcement |
| Problem | Customer pain point |
| Solution | Proposed capability |
| Benefits | Customer outcomes |
| FAQ | Risks & details |

---

# Example

## Product
AI Meeting Assistant

### Press Release
“Today we launched an AI assistant that automatically summarizes meetings and extracts action items.”

### FAQ
- How accurate is summarization?
- What privacy controls exist?
- What integrations are supported?

---

# Best Used When

- New product proposals
- Executive reviews
- Strategic initiatives
- Innovation planning

---

# Advantages

- Encourages deep thinking
- Clarifies customer value
- Improves stakeholder alignment
- Reduces vague ideas

---

# Limitations

- Time-intensive initially
- Requires strong writing skills

---

# Best Practices

- Write for customers first
- Keep language simple
- Anticipate hard questions
- Focus on outcomes over features
