## 📦 4Ps → 7Ps Marketing Mix
📌 What is it?

The Marketing Mix is a framework used to design and evaluate how a product is positioned and delivered in the market.

Originally:

🎯 **4Ps (Product Marketing)**
1. Product
2. Price
3. Place
4. Promotion

Expanded to:

🚀 **7Ps (Service + Digital Economy)**
1. Product
2. Price
3. Place
4. Promotion

5. People
6. Process
7. Physical Evidence

👉 Used to understand how a business delivers value + experience

⏰ **When to Use It**

Use 4Ps/7Ps when:

- Designing a go-to-market (GTM) strategy
- Launching a new product or feature
- Evaluating product positioning
- Improving customer experience
- Working on marketing + growth strategy

👉 Best for: launch + growth planning

📍 **Where to Use It**
- 📣 GTM strategy documents
- 🧭 Product positioning work
- 📊 Marketing strategy planning
- 🏢 Startup business model design
- 🚀 Launch planning decks

📊 **Example (Food Delivery App – Swiggy/Zomato)**
🍔 7Ps Breakdown

Element	Example

- Product	Food delivery app, tracking, offers

- Price	Delivery fees, surge pricing, discounts

- Place	Mobile app (Android/iOS), web app

- Promotion	Coupons, ads, referral codes

- People	Delivery partners, customer support

- Process	Order → restaurant → delivery workflow

- Physical Evidence	App UI, order tracking screen, packaging
