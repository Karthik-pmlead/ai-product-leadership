# PESTLE Framework

📌 **What is it?**

PESTLE is a macro-environment analysis framework used to understand external factors that affect a business or product.
🧠 Simple PM Interpretation
Political → rules of the game
Economic → money situation
Social → user behavior
Technological → what’s possible
Legal → what’s allowed
Environmental → sustainability & impact


⏰ **When to Use PESTLE**

Use PESTLE when:

Entering a new market or country
Doing product strategy planning
Evaluating long-term risks and opportunities
Launching a new product or expansion
Analyzing external forces affecting growth

👉 Best for: strategy, market entry, and high-level planning

📍**Where to Use It**
📊 Market research documents
🧭 Product strategy / vision docs
🌍 Geo expansion planning
💼 Business case preparation
🏢 Startup validation

| Factor | What it means | Key Question | Impact on Product/Business |
|--------|---------------|--------------|------------------------------|
| 🏛️ Political | Government policies, regulations, stability | How do laws and policies affect the market? | Can enable or restrict business operations |
| 💰 Economic | Inflation, income levels, GDP, interest rates | Is the market economically favorable? | Impacts pricing, demand, and purchasing power |
| 👥 Social | Culture, demographics, lifestyle trends | What are user behaviors and preferences? | Shapes product adoption and user needs |
| ⚙️ Technological | Innovation, automation, tech infrastructure | What technologies influence the market? | Enables new features and disrupts industries |
| ⚖️ Legal | Laws, compliance, data protection rules | What legal constraints exist? | Impacts risk, compliance, and product design |
| 🌱 Environmental | Sustainability, climate, ecological impact | How does environment affect business? | Influences operations and brand perception |

📊 **Example (Food Delivery App in India)**
Factor	Example Insight

🏛️ Political	Food safety regulations, delivery labor laws
💰 Economic	Inflation affects ordering frequency
👥 Social	Urban lifestyle → high demand for convenience food
⚙️ Technological	Growth of smartphones + UPI payments
⚖️ Legal	Data privacy laws, gig worker regulations
🌱 Environmental	Packaging waste concerns → push for eco packaging

🚀 **PM Insight**

PESTLE helps you:
Avoid blind expansion mistakes
Spot macro risks early
Identify big opportunity shifts
Build future-proof product strategy
