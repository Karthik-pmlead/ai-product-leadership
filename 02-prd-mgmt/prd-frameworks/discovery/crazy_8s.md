# Crazy 8s

## Overview

Crazy 8s is a rapid ideation exercise commonly used in:
- design sprints
- UX workshops
- product brainstorming

Participants sketch:
- 8 ideas
- in 8 minutes

The goal is to encourage fast thinking and creative exploration.

---

# Process

1. Fold paper into 8 sections
2. Set timer for 8 minutes
3. Sketch 1 idea per section
4. Focus on speed over perfection
5. Share and discuss ideas

---

# Core Principles

- Quantity over quality initially
- Avoid self-censorship
- Encourage divergent thinking
- Explore unconventional ideas

---

# Best Used When

- UX ideation
- Discovery workshops
- Design sprints
- Feature brainstorming
- Innovation sessions

---

# Advantages

- Fast and engaging
- Encourages creativity
- Prevents overthinking
- Produces many solution ideas

---

# Limitations

- Not ideal for deep analysis
- Some participants may dislike sketching
- Ideas may lack detail

---

# Example Prompt

```text
How might we improve mobile checkout experience?
```

Potential ideas:
- One-click purchase
- Voice checkout
- AI autofill
- Guest checkout

---

# Best Practices

- Keep prompts focused
- Encourage wild ideas
- Avoid critique during ideation
- Debrief after sketching
