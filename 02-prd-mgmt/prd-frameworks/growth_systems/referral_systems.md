# Referral Systems

## Overview

Referral Systems are growth mechanisms where existing users help acquire new users through:
- invitations
- sharing
- recommendations
- incentives

They are one of the most efficient customer acquisition channels because:
- trust transfers socially.

---

# Core Idea

```text
Existing Users
      ↓
Invite Others
      ↓
New Users Join
      ↓
More Referrals
```

Referral systems often create:
- compounding growth loops

---

# Common Referral Models

| Model | Example |
|---|---|

| Social Sharing | TikTok content sharing |
| Collaboration Invite | Slack workspace invites |
| Affiliate Referral | Creator commissions |
| Member-Get-Member | Fintech referral bonuses |

# Best Used When

- Product-led growth
- Consumer apps
- Collaboration tools
- Marketplace products
- Community-driven platforms

---

# Advantages

- Lower acquisition costs
- High-trust acquisition
- Strong scalability potential
- Encourages engagement

---

# Limitations

- Incentive abuse possible
- Weak products rarely spread
- Poor onboarding reduces conversion

---

# Best Practices

- Make sharing frictionless
- Reward both sides when appropriate
- Optimize invite onboarding
- Track referral quality, not volume alone

---

# Referral vs Virality

| Referral | Virality |
|---|---|
| Often intentional | Can be passive |
| Incentive-driven | Product-driven |
| Explicit sharing | Natural spread |
