# Product-Led Growth (PLG)

## Overview

Product-Led Growth (PLG) is a business and growth strategy where:
> the product itself drives acquisition, activation, retention, and expansion.

Instead of relying primarily on:
- sales teams
- outbound marketing

PLG companies use:
- product experience
- self-service onboarding
- virality
- user value

to fuel growth.

---

# Core Idea

Users experience value before making a purchasing decision.

The product becomes:
- the primary acquisition channel
- the main conversion engine
- the retention mechanism

---

# Typical PLG Flow

```text
User Discovers Product
        ↓
Self-Service Signup
        ↓
Fast Time-to-Value
        ↓
Habit Formation
        ↓
Expansion / Paid Conversion
        ↓
Referral & Virality
```

---

# Key Characteristics

| Characteristic | Description |
|---|---|
| Self-Service | Minimal sales dependency |
| Fast Activation | Quick value realization |
| Freemium / Trial | Low adoption friction |
| Viral Distribution | Sharing drives growth |
| Usage-Based Expansion | Product usage increases revenue |

---

# Common PLG Models

| Model | Example |
|---|---|
| Freemium | Slack |
| Free Trial | Notion |
| Usage-Based | Zoom |
| Open Core | GitLab |

---

# Key Metrics

| Metric | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Activation Rate | Users reaching first value |
| Time-to-Value | Speed to meaningful outcome |
| Product Qualified Leads (PQLs) | High-intent users |
| Expansion Revenue | Upsell/cross-sell growth |
| Retention | Long-term engagement |

---

# Best Used When

- SaaS products
- Collaboration tools
- Developer tools
- Consumer platforms
- Self-service products

---

# Advantages

- Lower acquisition costs
- Faster scalability
- Better user adoption
- Strong network effects possible

---

# Limitations

- Requires excellent UX
- Complex onboarding hurts PLG
- Enterprise sales may still be necessary
- Product quality becomes critical

---

# Example

## Slack

Growth driven through:
- team invitations
- self-service onboarding
- collaborative usage

The product itself spreads within organizations.

---

# Best Practices

- Optimize onboarding aggressively
- Reduce signup friction
- Focus on fast value delivery
- Instrument product analytics deeply
- Encourage collaboration & sharing
