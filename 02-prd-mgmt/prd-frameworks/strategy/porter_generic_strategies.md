# Porter Generic Strategies

## Overview

Porter’s Generic Strategies is a competitive strategy framework created by:
- Michael Porter

It explains how organizations achieve competitive advantage through:
- cost leadership
- differentiation
- focus strategies

---

# The Three Core Strategies

## Cost Leadership

Compete through:
- lowest operational cost
- economies of scale
- operational efficiency

Goal:
- offer lower prices profitably

Examples:
- Walmart
- Ryanair

---

## Differentiation

Compete through:
- unique value
- premium experience
- innovation
- branding

Examples:
- Apple
- Tesla

---

## Focus Strategy

Target:
- niche segments
- specialized markets

Two variants:
- Cost Focus
- Differentiation Focus

Examples:
- Luxury niche brands
- Enterprise SaaS tools

- Markets evolve rapidly
- Oversimplifies modern ecosystems
