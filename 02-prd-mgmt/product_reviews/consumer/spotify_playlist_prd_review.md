# Spotify Playlist — Product Strategy & PRD Review

## Overview

Spotify Playlists are one of Spotify’s strongest engagement and retention features, enabling users to discover, organize, and share personalized music experiences.

The playlist ecosystem combines:
- personalization
- recommendation intelligence
- social discovery
- creator participation
- emotional engagement

to drive long-term user retention and platform growth.
