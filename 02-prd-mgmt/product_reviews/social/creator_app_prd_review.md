## 2) Social: Creator-Led Social Video App

### Overview

This product is a creator-led social video platform where content discovery, community, and creator trust drive engagement. Social trends in 2026 favor short-form video, creator authenticity, and algorithmic discovery, so the product should optimize for fast attention capture and repeat engagement [web:77][web:81].

| Area | Review |
|---|---|
| Product | Creator-led social video and community app. |
| Core use case | Watch, create, share, and engage around short-form content. |
| Platform model | Multi-sided social platform with creators and viewers. |
| Primary value | Entertainment, identity, community, and discovery. |
| Review type | PRD review with engagement and network-effects emphasis. |

### Visual and Experience

The app should feel energetic, lightweight, and scroll-friendly. At the same time, it must preserve creator identity and community context so content feels human rather than generic or purely algorithmic [web:77][web:81].

| Visual Dimension | Assessment |
|---|---|
| Navigation | Extremely simple and discovery-led. |
| Information density | Low per screen, high across the feed. |
| Trust cues | Creator identity, audience interaction, and authenticity. |
| Consistency | Important across feed, creation, messaging, and profile. |
| UX priority | Fast discovery, easy creation, and sticky consumption. |

### Mission and Vision

| Element | Statement |
|---|---|
| Mission | Help creators build audience and help users discover meaningful content. |
| Vision | Turn social video into a creator-first community engine. |
| Strategic direction | Combine video, community, creator tools, and commerce. |
| Long-term ambition | Become the primary daily social layer for creator-led discovery. |

### Pain Points

| Pain Point | User Impact | Product Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Low creator retention | Weak content supply. | High. |
| Feed fatigue | Lower engagement. | High. |
| Poor moderation | Safety and trust issues. | High. |
| Weak monetization | Creator churn. | High. |
| Generic recommendations | Reduced relevance. | Medium. |

### Recommendations

| Recommendation | Why It Matters | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Build strong creator identity surfaces | Increases trust and loyalty. | High. |
| Improve recommendation quality by niche | Boosts relevance and retention. | High. |
| Add creator monetization early | Reduces churn among top creators. | High. |
| Strengthen moderation and reporting tools | Protects community trust. | High. |
| Support collaborative creation | Expands network effects. | Medium. |
| Add lightweight analytics for creators | Helps content iteration. | Medium. |

### Risks and Tradeoffs

| Risk | Tradeoff | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Stronger moderation | Slightly lower content velocity. | Better safety, more friction. |
| More creator controls | More complexity. | Better trust, harder UX. |
| More algorithmic ranking | Less creator transparency. | Better relevance, weaker trust if opaque. |
| More monetization options | More product clutter. | Better retention, harder simplicity. |
