# Executive Summary

Palantir AIP is not simply another Generative AI platform.

Its long-term vision is to build an **Enterprise Decision Operating System**—a platform that connects enterprise data, operational context, AI reasoning, workflows, and human oversight to improve organizational decision-making.

Unlike AI copilots that primarily generate content or answer questions, Palantir focuses on helping organizations make operational decisions and execute them safely.

The product demonstrates an important evolution in Enterprise AI:

```
Data

↓

Analytics

↓

Predictions

↓

Recommendations

↓

Decision Intelligence

↓

Enterprise Operating System
```

Its competitive advantage comes not from language models alone but from enterprise ontology, workflow orchestration, governance, and operational context.
