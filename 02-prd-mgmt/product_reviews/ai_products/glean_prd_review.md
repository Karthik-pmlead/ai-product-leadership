# Executive Summary

Glean is often described as an enterprise search platform powered by AI.

That description is accurateâ€”but incomplete.

Its larger vision is to become the **Enterprise Knowledge Operating System**, enabling employees and AI agents to discover, understand, and reuse organizational knowledge securely across hundreds of enterprise applications.
