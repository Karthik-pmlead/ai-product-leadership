# Executive Summary

Microsoft 365 Copilot is often described as an AI assistant embedded into Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, and PowerPoint.

That description is accurate—but incomplete.

Its broader vision is to become the **AI Operating System for Knowledge Work**, helping employees navigate organizational knowledge, reduce cognitive load, coordinate work across applications, and make better decisions.

Unlike traditional productivity software that focuses on creating documents, Microsoft 365 Copilot focuses on orchestrating work across an organization's communication, collaboration, and knowledge ecosystem.

Its long-term competitive advantage comes not from language models alone, but from Microsoft Graph, enterprise context, workflow integration, identity, and organizational intelligence.

Traditional productivity software asks:
