# Executive Summary

Bloomberg Terminal is often described as a market data terminal.

That description significantly understates its impact.

Bloomberg fundamentally changed how financial professionals work by integrating real-time market data, news, analytics, research, communication, and trading workflows into a single platform.

Long before the rise of AI, Bloomberg demonstrated a principle that defines today's most successful enterprise products:

> **The greatest products don't simply provide informationâ€”they enable better decisions.**

Today, as financial institutions adopt Generative AI, Bloomberg's decades of proprietary financial data, trusted workflows, and institutional relationships position it as one of the strongest examples of an AI-ready enterprise platform.
