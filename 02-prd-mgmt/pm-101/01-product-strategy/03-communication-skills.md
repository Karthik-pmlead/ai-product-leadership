#### Negotiations
  - As a PM, choosing the right conflict style depends on urgency, stakes, and relationships.

| Style         | Description                                      | When to Use                                  | Risk / Downside                         |
|---------------|--------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Compromise    | Both sides give up something to reach a middle   | When time is limited and stakes are moderate  | No one fully satisfied                 |
| Avoid         | Ignore or delay the conflict                     | When issue is trivial or emotions are high    | Problem may resurface later            |
| Competitive   | One side wins, the other loses                   | When quick, decisive action is needed         | Can damage relationships               |
| Collaborative | Work together to find a win-win solution         | When long-term relationship matters           | Time-consuming                         |
| Accommodate   | One side yields to maintain harmony              | When relationship matters more than outcome   | Own needs may be ignored               |
