## My Approach
My 5-step consulting methodology, refined from 50+ client projects.

PROBLEM → STRUCTURE → ANALYZE → SYNTHESIZE → RECOMMEND

| Step | Key Questions | Tools/Frameworks | Template |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **1. Problem Definition** | What exactly are we solving? | Issue Tree | [Template](my-approach/step1-problem.md) |
| **2. Structure** | How do we break this down? | MECE, Hypothesis | [Template](my-approach/step2-structure.md) |
| **3. Analyze** | What data do we need? | Profitability, 4Cs | [Template](my-approach/step3-analyze.md) |
| **4. Synthesize** | What does it all mean? | Pyramid Principle | [Template](my-approach/step4-synthesize.md) |
| **5. Recommend** | What should we do next? | Actionable roadmap | [Template](my-approach/step5-recommend.md) |
