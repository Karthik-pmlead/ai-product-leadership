# Leadership Knowledge Base

Welcome to the **Leadership Knowledge Base**, a curated collection of practices, frameworks, and playbooks for **Engineering Managers (EMs), Directors, and VPs**.  

This repository is designed to:
- Clarify what “good” leadership looks like at each level (EM → Director → VP).  
- Provide reusable formats, examples, and decision‑making templates.  
- Help you scale your impact from managing a team to managing teams of teams and shaping technology strategy.

Each folder contains concise guides, checklists, and examples you can adapt to your context.

## How to use this repo

- **EMs**: Start with `05‑people`, `04‑communication`, `03‑okr`, and `06‑results`, then explore `09‑feedback‑coaching` and `02‑engg`.  
- **Directors**: Lean into `07‑strategy`, `12‑scale‑systems`, and `08‑decision‑making` while reinforcing `01‑cross‑functional` and `10‑conflict‑influence`.  
- **VPs**: Use `07‑strategy`, `12‑scale‑systems`, and `11‑culture‑values` as your primary anchors, and treat the rest as enablers for your org.

PRs and feedback are welcome—this is a living guide meant to evolve with your leadership journey.
