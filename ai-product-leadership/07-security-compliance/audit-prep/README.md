# Audit‑Prep – Security & Compliance Audit Preparation

This folder (`security-compliance/audit-prep/`) contains **checklists and playbooks** for preparing for:
- GDPR‑style data‑protection‑audits,  
- ISO‑27001‑style information‑security audits,  
- CE‑Marking‑style product‑safety and risk‑assessment audits.

These materials are designed for:
- Product‑leaders who own product‑level evidence.  
- Engineering and security‑leads who own technical‑evidence and logs.  

The goal is to treat **audit‑prep as a repeatable product‑process**, not a one‑off panic‑cycle.
