# Risk Management – For Product & Security Leaders

This folder (`security-compliance/risk-management/`) contains a **lightweight risk‑management framework** for product‑teams, aligned with:
- GDPR‑style data‑risk,
- ISO‑27001‑style information‑security risk,
- OWASP‑Top‑10‑style technical‑risk,
- CE‑Marking‑style product‑safety and risk‑assessment.

The goal is to show how **risk‑thinking** can be embedded into product‑design, SDLC, and incident‑management, not treated as a one‑off compliance exercise.
