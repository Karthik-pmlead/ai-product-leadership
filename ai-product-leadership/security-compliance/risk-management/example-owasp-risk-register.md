# Example OWASP‑Style Risk Register

This file shows an **OWASP‑aligned risk register** for a web and API‑based product (e.g., SaaS platform). Risks are mapped to **OWASP‑Top‑10‑style categories** and treated as backlog items for the product‑team.

## How this ties into product‑work

- **Product‑leaders** can treat this as a **security‑debt backlog**, prioritising high‑ and critical‑risk items (OWASP‑001, OWASP‑002, OWASP‑003) alongside feature‑epics.  
- **Engineering teams** can reference `OWASP Category` when performing threat‑modeling and secure‑design reviews.  
- **Security‑leads** can present this register in **ISO‑27001‑style discussions** as evidence of systematic risk‑assessment and remediation planning.
