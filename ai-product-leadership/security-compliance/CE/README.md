# CE‑Marking & Product‑Safety for AI‑Product Leaders

This folder (`ai-product-leadership/security-compliance/CE/`) collects material on **CE‑Marking‑style thinking** for software‑and‑IoT‑products, especially for product leaders and engineering teams in IoT‑manufacturing and connected‑device contexts.

---

## What this collection is for

- **Product leaders** who ship **IoT, embedded, or cyber‑physical products** (e.g., smart locks, sensors, controllers) that must comply with **CE‑Marking‑style safety and regulatory expectations**.  
- **Engineering and security leaders** who translate **CE‑Marking‑style risk‑assessment and documentation** into product‑design and SDLC practices.  

The goal is to show how **product‑safety and risk‑thinking** (typically applied to hardware) can be extended to **software‑and‑security components** in an AI‑product context.
