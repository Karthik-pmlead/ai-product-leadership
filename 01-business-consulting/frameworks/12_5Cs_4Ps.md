# 5Cs and 4Ps Marketing Framework

This framework combines **external and internal analysis (5Cs)** with **marketing execution (4Ps)**, providing a structured approach for strategy development.

---
### How to Use the Framework
1. Perform 5Cs Analysis
  - Map internal strengths, customer needs, competitive landscape, partners, and market context
2. Derive Marketing Mix
  - Translate insights into Product, Price, Place, and Promotion strategies
3. Evaluate & Iterate
  - Test assumptions, assess alignment with objectives, monitor results
4. Integrate with KPIs
  - Product: adoption rate, return
  - Price: margin, sales growth
  - Place: coverage, channel efficiency
  - Promotion: engagement, conversion

---
## Step 1: 5Cs Analysis

The **5Cs** help you assess the environment and context before defining marketing strategies.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    FiveC[5Cs Analysis] --> Company[Company]
    FiveC --> Customers[Customers]
    FiveC --> Competitors[Competitors]
    FiveC --> Collaborators[Collaborators]
    FiveC --> Context[Context]

    %% Company
    Company --> Strengths[Strengths & Capabilities]
    Company --> Weaknesses[Weaknesses & Gaps]
    Company --> Resources[Financial & Human Resources]

    %% Customers
    Customers --> Segments[Customer Segments]
    Customers --> Needs[Needs & Preferences]
    Customers --> Behavior[Buying Behavior & Decision Drivers]

    %% Competitors
    Competitors --> Direct[Direct Competitors]
    Competitors --> Indirect[Indirect/Substitute Competitors]
    Competitors --> Positioning[Market Positioning & Strategy]

    %% Collaborators
    Collaborators --> Suppliers[Suppliers & Vendors]
    Collaborators --> Partners[Distribution / Strategic Partners]
    Collaborators --> Channels[Channel Effectiveness]

    %% Context
    Context --> MarketTrends[Market Trends & Growth]
    Context --> Regulations[Regulatory Environment]
    Context --> Technology[Technology & Innovation]
```
