# Due Diligence Framework

## Objective
Structured framework for evaluating a company, investment, acquisition target, vendor, or strategic partnership opportunity.

---

# Due Diligence Areas

| Area | Key Questions | Key Outputs |
|---|---|---|
| Commercial | Is the market attractive? | Growth outlook |
| Financial | Are the financials healthy? | Revenue quality |
| Operational | Can the business scale? | Operational gaps |
| Product & Tech | Is the technology defensible? | Technical assessment |
| Legal & Compliance | Are there legal risks? | Risk exposure |
| HR & Organization | Is leadership capable? | Talent assessment |

---
