# Entering New Market Framework

This framework guides companies through **strategic market entry**, ensuring a structured and MECE approach.

---

Entering New Market

### How to Use
1. Assess each potential market using Market Assessment and Competitive Analysis
2. Score and prioritize using a Decision Matrix
3. Design entry strategy tailored to the chosen market(s)
4. Iterate based on real-time intelligence and feedback

---


```mermaid
flowchart LR
    Entry[Entering New Market] --> MarketAssessment[Market Assessment]
    Entry --> CompetitiveAnalysis[Competitive Analysis]

    %% Market Assessment
    MarketAssessment --> Size[Market Size]
    MarketAssessment --> Growth[Growth Rate]
    MarketAssessment --> Segments[Key Segments]
    MarketAssessment --> Trends[Trends & Future Outlook]

    %% Competitive Analysis
    CompetitiveAnalysis --> Players[Existing Competitors]
    CompetitiveAnalysis --> MarketShare[Market Share Distribution]
    CompetitiveAnalysis --> Strengths[Competitor Strengths / Weaknesses]
    CompetitiveAnalysis --> Barriers[Barriers to Entry]

```

## Step 3: Decision Matrix

Prioritize markets based on **attractiveness** and **feasibility**.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    DecisionMatrix[Decision Matrix] --> Attractiveness[Market Attractiveness]
    DecisionMatrix --> Feasibility[Entry Feasibility]
    DecisionMatrix --> Prioritization[Rank & Prioritize Markets]
```

#### Summary

This Entering New Market Framework ensures a structured approach to:

 - Evaluate market attractiveness
 - Understand competitive dynamics
 - Prioritize markets through a decision matrix
Execute entry strategy effectively and efficiently
