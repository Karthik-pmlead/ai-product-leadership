# 🧑‍💼 Hiring System

> Structured approach to hiring high-quality talent consistently.

---

# 📌 Focus Areas

- Role definition (skills, level, scope)
- Interview loops
- Assessment rubrics
- Hiring scorecards
- Bar raiser alignment

---

# 🧭 Hiring Flow

Role Definition → Sourcing → Screening → Technical Rounds → Behavioral Round → Hiring Decision

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Hire for potential + impact
- Structured interviews reduce bias
- Every hire must raise team bar
- Use scorecards, not opinions

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Consistent, high-quality hiring decisions.
