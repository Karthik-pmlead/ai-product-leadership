# 🤝 Retention & Engagement

> Building a healthy, motivated, and stable team environment.

---

# 📌 Focus Areas

- Engagement surveys
- Team health metrics
- Recognition systems
- Burnout monitoring
- Career satisfaction

---

# 📊 Signals

| Signal | Indicator |
|---|---|
| Engagement | Survey scores |
| Burnout | Workload imbalance |
| Attrition risk | Low engagement + low growth |
| Morale | Team sentiment |

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Retention is proactive, not reactive
- Recognition must be frequent
- Burnout is a system issue, not individual failure
- Growth drives retention

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Healthy, motivated, and stable teams.
