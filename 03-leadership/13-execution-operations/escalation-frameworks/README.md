# 🚨 Escalation Frameworks

> Structured approach to resolving blockers quickly and predictably.

---

# 📊 Escalation Levels

| Level | Response Time |
|---|---|
| Team level | <24 hrs |
| Program level | <48 hrs |
| Executive level | <72 hrs |

---

# 🧭 Escalation Triggers

- Cross-team blockers
- Missed milestones
- Risk of OKR failure
- Resource constraints
- Critical production issues

---

# 📌 Rules

- Every escalation must have an owner
- Every escalation must have a timestamp
- Every escalation must have resolution path

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Faster resolution of blockers with minimal delivery disruption.
