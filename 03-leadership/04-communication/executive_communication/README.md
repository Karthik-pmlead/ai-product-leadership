# 🧑‍💼 Executive Communication

> High-signal communication for leadership, strategy, and organizational alignment.

---

# 📌 Focus Areas

- CEO / VP updates
- Board updates
- Strategic narratives
- Quarterly business reviews
- Risk & escalation updates

---

# 🧭 Structure

Context → Key Insights → Risks → Decisions Needed → Recommendations

---

# 🧠 Principles

- High signal, low noise
- Focus on decisions, not activity
- Use metrics and outcomes
- Be concise and structured

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Fast executive clarity and decision-making.
