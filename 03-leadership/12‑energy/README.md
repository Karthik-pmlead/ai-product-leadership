# ⚡ Energy & Execution Sustainability System

> A leadership system for managing team energy, preventing burnout, and sustaining high performance over time.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

This module ensures:
- Sustainable execution pace across teams
- Burnout prevention mechanisms
- Healthy workload distribution
- Long-term productivity stability
- Balanced innovation vs delivery pressure

# 🧠 Core Principles

- Velocity without sustainability is failure
- Burnout is a system issue, not individual weakness
- Energy is a constrained resource
- Rest is part of performance design
- Sustainable teams outperform heroic teams

> High-performing teams that remain healthy over time.
