# 💚 Team Health

> Measuring and improving team morale, engagement, and psychological safety.

---

# 📌 Health Indicators

- Engagement survey scores
- Team sentiment (qualitative)
- Attrition risk signals
- Collaboration quality
- Meeting fatigue levels

---

# 🧭 Health Model

Observe → Measure → Diagnose → Intervene → Improve

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Healthy teams ship better products
- Psychological safety is non-negotiable
- Feedback loops must be continuous
- Culture is a system output

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> High-trust, high-performance teams.
