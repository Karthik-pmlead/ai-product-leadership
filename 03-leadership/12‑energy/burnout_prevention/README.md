# 🛑 Burnout Prevention

> Systems to detect, prevent, and mitigate team burnout early.

---

# 📌 Burnout Signals

| Signal | Indicator |
|---|---|
| Overcommitment | Consistently missed deadlines |
| Fatigue | Reduced velocity over time |
| Context switching | High multitasking load |
| Morale drop | Engagement decline |

---

# 🧭 Prevention Mechanisms

- Sustainable sprint planning
- Mandatory buffer allocation
- Rotation of high-stress tasks
- Regular retrospectives

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Burnout is predictable, not sudden
- Load distribution must be intentional
- Recovery time is mandatory
- Leaders own system health

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Reduced burnout risk and improved team longevity.
