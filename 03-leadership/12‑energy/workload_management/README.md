# ⚖️ Workload Management

> Balancing execution demand with team capacity to prevent overload and inefficiency.

---

# 📌 Focus Areas

- Sprint capacity planning
- Task prioritization balance
- Work-in-progress (WIP) limits
- Cross-team load balancing

---

# 🧭 Workload Model

Capacity =  
Committed work (70%) + Innovation (20%) + Buffer (10%)

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Never operate at 100% utilization
- Protect buffer capacity
- Avoid WIP overload
- Prioritize finishing over starting

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Predictable delivery without overload.
