# 🧠 Psychological Safety

> Creating environments where individuals can speak up without fear of negative consequences.

---

# 📊 Key Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| Voice safety | Ability to raise concerns |
| Risk safety | Freedom to experiment |
| Error safety | Ability to admit mistakes |
| Inclusion safety | Feeling respected in group |

---

# 🧭 Leadership Behaviors

- Encourage dissenting opinions
- Normalize failure discussions
- Reward transparency over perfection
- Ask “what are we missing?”

---

# ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- Blame-driven culture
- Silent disagreement
- Fear of escalation
- Hero culture

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Teams speak early, fail safely, and recover faster.
