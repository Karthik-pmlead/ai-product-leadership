# 🌍 Inclusion & Belonging

> Creating an environment where diverse perspectives are included, respected, and leveraged.

---

# 📊 Focus Areas

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| Representation | Diverse team composition |
| Voice inclusion | Equal participation |
| Fairness | Bias-aware decision making |
| Accessibility | Inclusive systems & tools |

---

# 🧭 Practices

- Rotate meeting facilitators
- Encourage equal voice in discussions
- Document decisions transparently
- Review bias in hiring & promotion

---

# ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- Dominant voice bias
- Exclusion from decisions
- Informal networks driving outcomes

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Teams where every voice contributes to better decisions.
