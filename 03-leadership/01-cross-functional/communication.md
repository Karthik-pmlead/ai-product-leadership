# Communication

> Ensuring consistent, structured, and transparent communication across teams and leadership layers.

---

# 🧭 Communication Layers

## 1. Team Level
- Daily standups
- Sprint updates
- Task-level clarity

## 2. Cross-Team Level
- Weekly syncs
- Dependency reviews
- Roadmap alignment

## 3. Leadership Level
- Executive updates
- KPI dashboards
- Decision summaries

---

# 📦 Communication Artifacts

- Status reports
- Decision logs
- Risk updates
- Roadmap summaries

---

# 📊 Communication Principles

- Be concise and structured
- Prefer written communication
- No ambiguity in ownership
- Always include next steps

---

# ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- Over-reliance on meetings
- Missing written summaries
- Unclear action items
- Delayed escalation
