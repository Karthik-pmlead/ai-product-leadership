# Cross-Functional Leadership Overview

> Driving alignment and execution across engineering, product, design, and business teams to deliver shared outcomes.

---

# 🎯 Core Idea

Cross-functional leadership ensures that:
- Work is aligned across teams
- Dependencies are visible and managed early
- Execution is coordinated, not siloed
- Outcomes are shared, not functionally owned

---

# 🧭 Operating Principles

- Outcome > output
- Alignment before execution
- Visibility reduces friction
- Clear ownership (DRIs)
- Decisions documented, not assumed

---

# 📊 Success Signals

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Dependency delays | <10% of delivery |
| Cross-team alignment | >90% |
| Escalation resolution time | <48 hrs |
