# Alignment

> Ensuring all teams work toward the same goals, priorities, and success metrics.

---

# 🧭 Alignment Layers

## 1. Strategic Alignment
- Company OKRs → Team OKRs
- Quarterly priorities
- Clear success definitions

## 2. Product Alignment
- Roadmap sync across teams
- Shared feature priorities
- Trade-off decisions

## 3. Execution Alignment
- Sprint goals mapped to OKRs
- Shared backlog visibility
- Dependency mapping

---

# 📌 Key Practices

- Single source of truth for priorities
- OKR cascade model
- Quarterly planning cycles
- Weekly alignment reviews

---

# ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- Conflicting team priorities
- Undefined ownership
- Late-stage priority changes
- Misaligned success metrics
