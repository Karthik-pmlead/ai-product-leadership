# 🚀 cross-functional/execution.md

# Execution

> Coordinating delivery across multiple teams to ensure predictable and high-quality outcomes.

---

# 🔄 Execution Flow

Plan → Align → Execute → Track → Adjust → Deliver

---

# 📦 Execution Components

## 1. Planning
- Shared roadmap
- Capacity alignment
- Dependency identification

## 2. Delivery
- Sprint execution
- Cross-team syncs
- Blocker management

## 3. Tracking
- Weekly progress reviews
- KPI monitoring
- Risk tracking

---

# 📊 Execution Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Milestone hit rate | >90% |
| Sprint predictability | >90% |
| Blocker resolution time | <48 hrs |

---

# 🧠 Key Behaviors

- Proactively surface risks
- Track dependencies early
- Escalate fast, not late
- Focus on outcomes, not activity
