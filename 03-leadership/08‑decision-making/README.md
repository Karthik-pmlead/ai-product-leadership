# 🧠 Decision Making

> Structured frameworks for making fast, high-quality, and reversible decisions at scale.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

This section defines how leaders:
- Make decisions under uncertainty
- Balance speed vs accuracy
- Document trade-offs
- Ensure accountability and clarity
- Reduce decision fatigue across orgs

---

# 🧠 Core Principles

- Speed > perfection (when reversibility is high)
- Document decisions, not just outcomes
- Make trade-offs explicit
- Assign clear ownership (DRI)
- Avoid re-litigating decisions repeatedly

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Faster, clearer, and more accountable decision systems across teams.
