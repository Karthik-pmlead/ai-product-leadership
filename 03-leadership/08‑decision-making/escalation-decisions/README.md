# 🚨 Escalation Decisions

> High-stakes decisions that require leadership or executive input.

---

# 📊 When to Escalate

- Cross-org conflicts
- Strategic roadmap changes
- Resource constraints across teams
- High business impact uncertainty
- Missed critical milestones

---

# 🧭 Escalation Flow

1. Prepare structured 1-pager
2. Define options (max 3)
3. Provide recommendation
4. Highlight risks & impact
5. Escalate to decision owner

---

# 📄 Required Format

- Problem statement
- Context
- Options
- Recommendation
- Impact analysis
- Decision required by

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Escalate with recommendation, not questions
- Time-box decisions
- Avoid repeated escalations for same issue

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Fast executive decisions with structured inputs and minimal ambiguity.
