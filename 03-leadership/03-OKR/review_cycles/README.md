# 🔄 OKR Review Cycles

> Structured cadence for evaluating OKR progress and adjusting execution.

---

# 📅 Cadence

| Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Weekly | Progress check |
| Bi-weekly | Risk & blockers |
| Quarterly | Full OKR review |

---

# 📌 Principles

- Focus on outcomes, not activity
- Identify risks early
- Adjust plans, not goals

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Continuous alignment and adaptive execution.
