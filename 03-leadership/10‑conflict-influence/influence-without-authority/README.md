# 🎯 Influence Without Authority

> Driving outcomes and decisions without direct control over teams or resources.

---

# 🧭 Influence Channels

| Channel | Example |
|---|---|
| Data | Metrics, KPIs, experiments |
| Narrative | Clear storytelling |
| Relationships | Trust-building across teams |
| Alignment | Shared goals and OKRs |
| Timing | Knowing when to escalate |

---

# 📌 Influence Model

1. Understand stakeholder priorities  
2. Align on shared outcomes  
3. Present data-backed recommendations  
4. Reduce ambiguity  
5. Reinforce through consistency  

---

# 🧠 Key Behaviors

- Lead with clarity, not urgency
- Frame decisions in business impact
- Build trust before pushing decisions
- Anticipate objections early

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Decisions move forward even without formal authority.
