# 🧭 Team Topologies

> Structuring teams based on ownership, flow, and cognitive load.

---

# 📌 Team Types

| Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Stream-aligned | Product delivery |
| Platform | Internal enablement |
| Enabling | Capability building |
| Complicated subsystem | Specialized expertise |

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Reduce cognitive load per team
- Align teams to product streams
- Minimize dependencies
- Enable fast flow of work

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Autonomous teams with minimal coordination overhead.
