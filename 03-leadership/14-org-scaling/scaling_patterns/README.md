# 📈 Scaling Patterns

> Proven and anti-patterns for growing organizations effectively.

---

# 📌 Scaling Patterns

## 1. Horizontal Scaling
- Add teams per product line

## 2. Vertical Scaling
- Add layers of leadership

## 3. Platform Scaling
- Centralize shared capabilities

## 4. Federation Model
- Autonomous teams with shared standards

---

# ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- Over-layered management
- Shared ownership ambiguity
- Excessive coordination meetings
- Unclear decision rights

---

# 🧠 Principles

- Scale should preserve speed
- Complexity must be actively managed
- Structure should evolve with product

---

# 🎯 Outcome

> Sustainable and controlled organizational growth.
