# 🧠 Core Principles

- Strategy is about choices, not plans
- Focus creates advantage
- Say “no” more than “yes”
- Long-term > short-term optimization
- Align execution to a single direction

# 🎯 Outcome

> Clear, defensible direction for product and business growth.
