# Enterprise AI Landscape

## Executive Summary

The largest AI opportunity within financial institutions may ultimately be internal productivity.

Large institutions contain vast amounts of fragmented knowledge.

---

# Enterprise Challenges

Information is distributed across:

- Documents
- Wikis
- Research
- Emails
- Internal systems

Knowledge retrieval remains inefficient.

---

# AI Opportunity

Enterprise AI Assistants

Capabilities:

- Knowledge retrieval
- Workflow automation
- Policy guidance
- Document generation
