# Financial AI Industry Overview

## Executive Summary

Financial Services is undergoing a once-in-a-generation platform transition driven by Artificial Intelligence.

Historically, financial institutions competed through:

- Capital
- Distribution
- Proprietary Information
- Human Expertise

The emergence of AI is creating a new source of competitive advantage:

Decision Intelligence.

The firms that successfully augment human decision-making with AI will increasingly outperform those relying solely on traditional workflows.
