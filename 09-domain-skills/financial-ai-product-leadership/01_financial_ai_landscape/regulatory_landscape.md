# Regulatory Landscape for Financial AI

## Executive Summary

AI adoption in finance is constrained by trust, explainability, governance, and regulation.

The most successful AI platforms will balance innovation with control.

---

# Major Regulatory Concerns

## Explainability

Users must understand:

- Why recommendations were generated
- Supporting evidence
- Confidence levels

---

## Model Risk Management

Requirements:

- Validation
- Monitoring
- Documentation

---

## Data Privacy

Requirements:

- Access controls
- Data lineage
- Security

---

## Compliance

Requirements:

- Auditability
- Record retention
- Human oversight

---

# Product Design Principles

Every financial AI platform should provide:

- Explainability
- Traceability
- Governance
- Human approval workflows

---

# Strategic Insight

Governance will become a competitive advantage rather than a compliance burden.
