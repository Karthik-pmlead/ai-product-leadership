# Buy Side vs Sell Side AI Landscape

## Executive Summary

AI adoption differs significantly between buy-side and sell-side institutions.

Understanding this distinction is critical for product strategy.

---

# Sell Side

Examples:

- Goldman Sachs
- JPMorgan
- Morgan Stanley

Primary Revenue Sources:

- Trading
- Market Making
- Investment Banking
- Prime Brokerage

Key Users:

- Traders
- Research Analysts
- Bankers
- Sales Teams

AI Opportunities:

- Research copilots
- Market intelligence
- Trading intelligence
- Client engagement
- Internal productivity

Primary Goal:

Increase revenue generation and operational efficiency.

---

# Buy Side

Examples:

- BlackRock
- Fidelity
- Vanguard
- Capital Group

Primary Revenue Sources:

- Asset Management Fees
- Advisory Fees

Key Users:

- Portfolio Managers
- Analysts
- Risk Teams

AI Opportunities:

- Investment research
- Portfolio optimization
- Risk forecasting
- Alternative data analysis

Primary Goal:

Improve investment outcomes.

---

# Product Implications

Sell Side AI focuses on:

- Decision speed
- Workflow efficiency
- Revenue acceleration

Buy Side AI focuses on:

- Decision quality
- Alpha generation
- Portfolio performance

---

# Strategic Insight

Future AI platforms will increasingly blur the line between buy-side and sell-side intelligence systems.
