# Marquee Platform Analysis (Goldman Sachs)

## 1. Product Overview

Marquee is Goldman Sachs’ institutional client platform for:

- Market research
- Data analytics
- Investment insights
- Trading signals
- Portfolio intelligence

It acts as a **central intelligence layer for institutional investors**.

---

## 2. Core User

Primary users include:

- Portfolio Managers
- Hedge Fund Analysts
- Institutional Investors
- Risk Managers

Their core workflow:

> Transforming fragmented market data into investment decisions.

---

## 3. Key User Problem

Institutional investors face:

- Excessive data fragmentation
- High research overhead
- Slow synthesis of insights
- Difficulty connecting macro + micro signals
- Lack of real-time decision intelligence

---

## 4. Current Product Paradigm

Today Marquee primarily functions as:

- Data + research aggregation platform
- Analytics interface
- Signal distribution system

However, it still relies heavily on:

> User-driven synthesis of insights.

---

## 5. AI Transformation Opportunity

Marquee evolves from:

> "Research Access Platform"

to:

> "AI Investment Intelligence System"

---

## 6. AI-Enabled Capabilities

### 6.1 Research Synthesis Engine
- Summarize macro + micro research
- Generate investment narratives
- Connect cross-asset signals

---

### 6.2 Portfolio Intelligence Layer
- Portfolio-level risk insights
- Exposure analysis
- Scenario modeling

---

### 6.3 Investment Copilot
- Query-based investment reasoning
- “What changed in markets today?”
- “How does inflation impact my portfolio?”

---

### 6.4 Multi-Agent Research System
- Market agent
- Risk agent
- Macro agent
- Sector agent

Outputs unified investment insights.

---

## 7. Key Design Requirement

All AI outputs must be:

- Grounded in data
- Explainable
- Source-backed
- Auditable

---

## 8. Strategic Insight

Marquee’s future value lies in becoming:

> The primary AI layer for institutional investment decision-making.
