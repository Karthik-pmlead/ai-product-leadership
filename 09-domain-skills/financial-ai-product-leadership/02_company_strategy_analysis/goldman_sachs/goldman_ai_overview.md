# Goldman Sachs AI Overview

## 1. Strategic Context

Goldman Sachs is uniquely positioned in financial services because it operates across:

- Global Markets (trading, execution, liquidity)
- Investment Banking (advisory, capital markets)
- Asset & Wealth Management (AUM, advisory services)
- Platform Solutions (payments, banking, embedded finance)

Across these businesses, AI is not a standalone capability.

It is an **enabling layer across decision-making, research, execution, and client engagement workflows**.

---

## 2. Core AI Opportunity

Goldman’s AI opportunity can be framed across three layers:

### 1. Intelligence Layer
- Market and research synthesis
- Investment insight generation
- Risk signal detection

### 2. Decision Layer
- Portfolio recommendations
- Trade execution optimization
- Advisor decision support

### 3. Workflow Layer
- Internal productivity copilots
- Research automation
- Compliance and reporting automation

---

## 3. Key Constraint Environment

Unlike consumer AI systems, Goldman AI must operate under:

- Regulatory constraints (auditability, compliance)
- High-stakes financial risk
- Model risk governance frameworks
- Strict explainability requirements
- Latency-sensitive environments (trading systems)

This makes Goldman AI fundamentally a:

> **Trusted Decision Intelligence System problem, not a generic LLM deployment problem.**

---

## 4. Strategic AI Thesis

Goldman AI strategy is best understood as:

> Embedding AI into high-value financial decision workflows while preserving trust, control, and accountability.

---

## 5. Primary AI Product Surface Areas

- Marquee (Research & Investment Intelligence)
- Trading & Execution Systems
- Wealth Advisory Platforms
- Internal Enterprise AI Assistants

These represent the four most important AI surface areas in the firm.

---

## 6. Key Strategic Insight

The long-term differentiator in financial AI will not be model capability.

It will be:

- Proprietary financial data
- Workflow integration depth
- Trust and governance systems
- Decision feedback loops

AI value compounds when embedded in workflows, not when exposed as tools.
