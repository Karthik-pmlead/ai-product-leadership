# Enterprise AI Assistants (Goldman Sachs)

## 1. Product Scope

Enterprise AI assistants focus on:

- Internal knowledge retrieval
- Workflow automation
- Document generation
- Compliance assistance
- Productivity enhancement

---

## 2. Core Users

- Analysts
- Bankers
- Traders
- Risk teams
- Operations teams

---

## 3. Core Problem

Employees face:

- Information fragmentation
- Time-intensive research
- Manual reporting workflows
- Repetitive documentation tasks

---

## 4. AI Opportunity

### 4.1 Knowledge Assistant (RAG systems)
- Internal document search
- Policy retrieval
- Research summarization

---

### 4.2 Workflow Copilot
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Generate reports
- Assist in analysis

---

### 4.3 Compliance-Aware AI
- Ensure outputs adhere to regulatory constraints
- Prevent unsafe or non-compliant generation

---

### 4.4 Agent-Based Systems
- Multi-step workflows
- Tool usage (data + APIs + documents)
- Task orchestration

---

## 5. Key Design Requirement

Enterprise AI must be:

- Permission-aware
- Audit-ready
- Secure by design
- Highly observable

---

## 6. Strategic Insight

Enterprise AI is the **foundation layer** that improves internal productivity and enables all other AI products to scale effectively.
