# Risks & Regulation — AI in Financial Systems

## 1. Core Risk Principle

In financial AI systems, the primary risk is not incorrect generation.

It is:

> Untraceable decision influence.

---

## 2. Key Risk Categories

### 2.1 Hallucination Risk
- Incorrect financial interpretation

Mitigation:
- Strict RAG grounding
- Source attribution enforcement

---

### 2.2 Compliance Risk
- Regulatory violations in investment guidance

Mitigation:
- Rule-based filtering
- Audit logs
- Human approval flows

---

### 2.3 Over-Reliance Risk
- Users over-trusting AI outputs

Mitigation:
- Confidence calibration
- Alternative perspectives
- Human-in-loop design

---

### 2.4 Data Privacy Risk
- Exposure of sensitive financial data

Mitigation:
- Role-based access control
- Encryption + secure retrieval

---

### 2.5 Model Drift Risk
- Changing market dynamics reduce accuracy

Mitigation:
- Continuous monitoring
- Feedback loops
- retraining pipelines

---

## 3. Regulatory Requirement

All AI outputs must be:

- Explainable
- Auditable
- Traceable to sources
- Deterministic in compliance behavior

---

## 4. Strategic Insight

In financial AI:

> Trust is not a feature — it is the product.
