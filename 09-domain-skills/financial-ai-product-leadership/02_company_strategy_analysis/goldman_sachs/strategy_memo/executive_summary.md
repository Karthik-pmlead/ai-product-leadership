# Executive Summary — Marquee AI Strategy

Goldman Sachs has a unique opportunity to transform Marquee from a research distribution platform into a **decision intelligence system for institutional investing**.

Today, institutional investors face high information density but low decision compression efficiency.

The core opportunity is to apply AI not to generate more insights, but to:

> Compress fragmented financial signals into structured, explainable, and actionable investment decisions.

This strategy proposes a unified AI transformation across research, portfolio intelligence, and workflow systems, centered on a Decision Compression Stack:

Signal Density → Context Compression → Multi-Perspective Reasoning → Confidence Calibration → Decision Actionability

The expected outcome is:

- Faster investment decision cycles
- Higher quality research utilization
- Improved client engagement with Marquee
- Stronger institutional retention through embedded AI workflows
