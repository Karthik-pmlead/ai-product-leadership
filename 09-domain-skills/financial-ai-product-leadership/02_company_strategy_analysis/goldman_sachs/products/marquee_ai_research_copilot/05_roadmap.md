# Roadmap — Marquee AI Research Copilot

## Phase 1: Foundation (0–3 months)

- RAG-based research summarization
- Basic query interface
- Market data integration
- Source citation system

---

## Phase 2: Intelligence Layer (3–6 months)

- Portfolio-aware responses
- Macro + micro synthesis
- Risk-aware insights
- Structured output generation

---

## Phase 3: Copilot Layer (6–12 months)

- Interactive multi-turn reasoning
- Investment thesis generation
- Scenario simulation
- Explanation engine

---

## Phase 4: Agentic System (12+ months)

- Multi-agent orchestration
- Autonomous research workflows
- Portfolio-level recommendations
- Continuous learning loop

---
