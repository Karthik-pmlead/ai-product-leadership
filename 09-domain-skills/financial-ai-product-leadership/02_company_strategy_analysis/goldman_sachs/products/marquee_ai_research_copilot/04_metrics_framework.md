# Metrics Framework — Marquee AI Research Copilot

## 1. Metric Philosophy

Traditional metrics (clicks, DAU) are insufficient.

Marquee AI must be evaluated based on:

> Decision quality, decision speed, and trust in outputs.

---

## 2. North Star Metric

### Decision Velocity Index

Measures:

> Time taken to go from question → investment decision-ready insight

---

## 3. Product Metrics

### 3.1 Adoption Metrics
- Weekly active institutional users
- Query frequency per user
- Repeat usage rate

---

### 3.2 Engagement Metrics
- Queries per session
- Depth of follow-up questions
- Multi-step analysis usage

---

### 3.3 Workflow Integration Metrics
- Insights saved to portfolio systems
- Insights shared across teams
- Copilot-influenced decisions

---

## 4. AI Quality Metrics

### 4.1 Groundedness Score
- % outputs backed by verified sources

---

### 4.2 Hallucination Rate
- Incorrect or unverifiable claims

---

### 4.3 Explanation Quality
- User-rated usefulness of reasoning

---

## 5. Business Metrics

- Trading volume influenced
- Research consumption uplift
- Client retention impact
- Revenue per institutional client

---

## 6. Risk Metrics

- Compliance violations
- Audit failure rate
- Model drift indicators

---

## 7. Key Insight

> The success of AI in financial systems is measured by decision outcomes, not model performance alone.
