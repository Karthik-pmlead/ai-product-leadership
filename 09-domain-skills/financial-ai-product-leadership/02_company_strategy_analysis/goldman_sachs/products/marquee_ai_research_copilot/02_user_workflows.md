# User Workflows — Marquee AI Research Copilot

## 1. Current Workflow (As-Is)

### Step 1: Information Gathering
- Open multiple research reports
- Check market data dashboards
- Review internal notes

### Step 2: Manual Synthesis
- Analyst reads across sources
- Extracts key insights manually
- Builds mental model of market

### Step 3: Portfolio Mapping
- Map insights to holdings
- Evaluate exposure and risk
- Form investment thesis

### Step 4: Decision Making
- PM validates thesis
- Executes trades or adjusts portfolio

---

## 2. Key Pain Points

- High cognitive load
- Fragmented information sources
- Slow synthesis cycle
- Lack of real-time portfolio-context alignment
- Heavy reliance on human interpretation

---

## 3. Future Workflow (To-Be with AI Copilot)

### Step 1: Natural Language Query
User asks:

> “What is the impact of rising yields on my portfolio?”

---

### Step 2: Context Injection
System automatically retrieves:
- Portfolio composition
- Market conditions
- Relevant research
- Macro indicators

---

### Step 3: AI Synthesis
Copilot generates:

- Structured summary
- Key drivers
- Risk exposure breakdown
- Scenario analysis

---

### Step 4: Explainable Output
Response includes:

- Evidence sources
- Market data references
- Portfolio-level impact
- Confidence score

---

### Step 5: Action Layer
User can:

- Save insight
- Share with team
- Trigger deeper analysis
- Adjust portfolio assumptions

---

## 4. Workflow Transformation

| Stage | Before | After |
|------|--------|------|
| Data Gathering | Manual | Automated |
| Insight Generation | Manual synthesis | AI-assisted reasoning |
| Portfolio Mapping | Separate step | Integrated |
| Decision Time | Hours | Minutes |

---

## 5. Key Insight

The primary value of AI is not replacing analysis — it is collapsing the distance between:

> Data → Insight → Decision
