# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Architecture Assessment

---

# 1. Executive Summary

The architecture enables a unified advisory intelligence system combining:

* client financial data
* market intelligence
* portfolio models
* AI reasoning layer
* compliance controls

---

# 2. System Architecture

```text id="wac1"
Client Data + Portfolio Data
        ↓
Data Aggregation Layer
        ↓
Wealth Knowledge Layer (RAG + Financial Graphs)
        ↓
AI Reasoning Layer (LLMs + Advisory Agents)
        ↓
Recommendation Engine
        ↓
Advisor Workflow Interface
        ↓
Compliance + Risk Layer
```

---

# 3. Core Components

---

## 3.1 Wealth Data Layer

* client portfolios
* holdings + transactions
* risk profiles
* financial goals

---

## 3.2 Knowledge Layer

* market research
* macroeconomic signals
* financial product knowledge
* regulatory constraints

---

## 3.3 AI Reasoning Layer

* portfolio analysis agents
* recommendation generation
* scenario simulation engines

---

## 3.4 Workflow Layer

* advisor dashboards
* client communication tools
* reporting automation

---

## 3.5 Compliance Layer

* suitability checks
* risk constraints
* audit logging
* regulatory reporting

---

# 4. Key Architectural Principles

---

## 4.1 Personalization-First Design

Every recommendation is client-specific and context-aware.

---

## 4.2 Compliance-by-Design

All AI outputs are constrained by regulatory suitability rules.

---

## 4.3 Human-in-the-Loop Advisory

Advisors remain final decision authority.

---

# 5. Key Risks

* incorrect personalization
* regulatory non-compliance
* hallucinated investment advice
* over-reliance on AI recommendations

---

# 6. Strategic Insight

> Wealth AI systems succeed only when they are both deeply personalized and strictly compliant.
