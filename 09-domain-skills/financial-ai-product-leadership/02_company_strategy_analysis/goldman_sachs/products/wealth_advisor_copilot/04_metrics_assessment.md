# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Metrics Assessment Framework

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Metrics evaluate performance across:

* advisory quality
* client outcomes
* advisor productivity
* compliance adherence

---

# 2. Core Metric Layers

---

## 2.1 Advisory Quality Metrics

* recommendation accuracy vs outcomes
* portfolio return improvement
* risk-adjusted performance uplift

---

## 2.2 Client Outcome Metrics

* client AUM growth
* retention rate
* satisfaction score (NPS)
* goal achievement rate

---

## 2.3 Advisor Productivity Metrics

* time per portfolio review
* number of clients handled per advisor
* advisory cycle time reduction

---

## 2.4 System Performance Metrics

* latency of portfolio analysis
* recommendation generation time
* system uptime

---

# 3. AI-Specific Metrics

---

## 3.1 Recommendation Quality

* suitability score alignment
* recommendation acceptance rate
* reversal rate after advice

---

## 3.2 Personalization Accuracy

* client-profile match score
* behavioral alignment index

---

## 3.3 RAG Performance

* retrieval relevance score
* citation correctness
* knowledge freshness

---

# 4. Business Impact Metrics

---

## 4.1 Revenue Impact

* AUM growth per advisor
* cross-sell effectiveness
* product penetration increase

---

## 4.2 Efficiency Gains

* reduced advisory time
* faster onboarding cycles
* automation rate in reporting

---

# 5. Strategic Insight

> In wealth management, AI success is measured not by prediction accuracy—but by client trust and advisor decision quality.

---

# 6. Final Recommendation

Adopt a unified wealth intelligence KPI stack linking:

* client outcomes
* advisor productivity
* recommendation quality
* compliance adherence
