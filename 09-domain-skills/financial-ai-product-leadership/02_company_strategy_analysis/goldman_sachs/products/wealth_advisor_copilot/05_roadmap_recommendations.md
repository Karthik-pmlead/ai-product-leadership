# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Roadmap Recommendations

---

# 1. Executive Summary

The roadmap evolves the Wealth Advisor Copilot from:

> advisory support tool → AI-assisted portfolio intelligence system → full advisory intelligence layer

---

# 2. Design Principles

* Compliance-first architecture
* Human-in-the-loop advisory model
* Personalization at scale
* Strong auditability and traceability
* Progressive AI augmentation (not disruption of advisor authority)

---

# 3. Roadmap Phases

---

# Phase 1 — Data & Advisory Foundation (0–3 months)

### Objective:

Build trusted financial and client data foundation

### Deliverables:

* unified client portfolio data model
* RAG-based financial knowledge system
* market data ingestion layer
* basic advisory summarization engine

### Outcome:

> Advisors get instant, grounded portfolio insights

---

# Phase 2 — Intelligent Advisory Layer (3–6 months)

### Objective:

Introduce AI-powered advisory support

### Deliverables:

* portfolio analysis engine
* risk profiling models
* recommendation generation system
* advisor copilot interface

### Outcome:

> AI begins assisting real advisory decisions

---

# Phase 3 — Personalized Wealth Intelligence (6–9 months)

### Objective:

Enable hyper-personalized recommendations

### Deliverables:

* client behavioral modeling
* goal-based investment planning
* tax-efficient strategy suggestions
* scenario simulation tools

### Outcome:

> advisory becomes deeply personalized and dynamic

---

# Phase 4 — Advisory Workflow Automation (9–12 months)

### Objective:

Automate repetitive advisory tasks

### Deliverables:

* automated reporting generation
* portfolio review summaries
* client communication drafts
* rebalancing alerts engine

### Outcome:

> advisors shift from analysis → decision focus

---

# Phase 5 — Next-Gen Wealth Intelligence System (12+ months)

### Objective:

Transform into full advisory intelligence operating layer

### Deliverables:

* continuous portfolio intelligence engine
* proactive advisory recommendations
* cross-client intelligence patterns
* agent-assisted advisory workflows

### Outcome:

> AI becomes continuous advisory co-pilot across all clients

---

# 4. Strategic Priorities

* Compliance-first expansion
* Advisor augmentation over replacement
* Trust-driven AI adoption
* Incremental automation of low-risk workflows

---

# 5. Key Insight

> In wealth management, adoption is not driven by capability alone—but by trust, compliance, and advisor control.
