# Marquee AI Research Copilot — Product Strategy

## 1. Product Vision

Marquee AI Research Copilot is an AI-powered intelligence layer that transforms fragmented financial research, market data, and portfolio context into:

> **trusted, explainable, and actionable investment insights for institutional investors.**

It evolves Marquee from a **data access platform** into a **decision intelligence system**.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Institutional investors face a structural bottleneck:

- Research is fragmented across reports, terminals, and internal notes
- Market data is high-volume and fast-changing
- Portfolio context is disconnected from research workflows
- Time-to-insight is slow and manual

As a result:

> Valuable signals exist, but decision latency remains high.

---

## 3. Target Users

### Primary Users
- Portfolio Managers (PMs)
- Hedge Fund Analysts
- Institutional Investors

### Secondary Users
- Risk Managers
- Macro Strategists
- Investment Banking Research Teams

---

## 4. Core Jobs To Be Done

Users are trying to:

- Understand “what changed in markets today?”
- Evaluate portfolio impact of macro events
- Synthesize multi-source research quickly
- Identify risks and opportunities early
- Validate investment theses with evidence

---

## 5. Product Vision

Marquee AI Copilot enables users to:

> Ask complex investment questions and receive grounded, explainable, multi-source investment intelligence.

Examples:

- “How does rising inflation impact my tech-heavy portfolio?”
- “Summarize key risks in European banking sector this week”
- “What are consensus vs non-consensus signals on AI semiconductors?”

---

## 6. Key Differentiator

Unlike generic LLM tools, Marquee AI Copilot is:

- Grounded in proprietary financial datasets
- Context-aware (portfolio + market + research)
- Explainable with citations
- Embedded into institutional workflows

---

## 7. Strategic Outcome

Move Marquee from:

> Passive research platform

to:

> Active AI-driven investment decision layer
