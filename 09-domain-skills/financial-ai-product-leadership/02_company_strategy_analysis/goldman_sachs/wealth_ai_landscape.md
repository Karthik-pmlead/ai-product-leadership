# Wealth AI Landscape (Goldman Sachs)

## 1. Product Scope

Wealth AI focuses on:

- Advisory intelligence
- Client portfolio insights
- Financial planning
- Personalized recommendations

---

## 2. Core Users

- Wealth advisors
- Relationship managers
- High-net-worth individuals (indirectly)

---

## 3. Core Problem

Wealth advisors face:

- Fragmented client data
- Manual portfolio analysis
- Time-intensive reporting
- Difficulty personalizing advice at scale

---

## 4. AI Opportunity

### 4.1 Client Intelligence Layer
- Unified client view
- Behavioral insights
- Portfolio summaries

---

### 4.2 Advisor Copilot
- Next-best-action recommendations
- Meeting preparation summaries
- Client risk alerts

---

### 4.3 Portfolio Narratives
- Explain portfolio performance in natural language
- Generate client-ready reports

---

## 5. Key Constraint

- High trust requirement
- Regulatory compliance
- Need for explainability
- Human advisor remains primary decision-maker

---

## 6. Strategic Insight

Wealth AI is not about replacing advisors.

It is about:

> Scaling high-quality advisory intelligence across client relationships.
