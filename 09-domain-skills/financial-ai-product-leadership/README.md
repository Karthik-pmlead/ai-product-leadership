## Executive Summary

This repository is a collection of product strategy, AI platform design, decision intelligence frameworks, and financial technology research developed to explore the future of AI-powered financial institutions.

The central thesis behind this work is simple:

> The future of finance will not be determined by who has the most information.
>
> It will be determined by who can transform information into high-quality decisions most effectively.

Across capital markets, asset management, wealth management, enterprise operations, and trading, organizations face a common challenge:

Information is abundant.

Decision capacity is scarce.

This repository explores how AI can be used to improve decision quality, accelerate execution, and create durable competitive advantages for financial institutions.

# Core Thesis

I believe financial institutions are evolving through three major eras:

### Era 1 — Record Systems

Purpose:

Store information.

Examples:

* Accounting systems
* Transaction systems
* Settlement systems

---

### Era 2 — Intelligence Systems

Purpose:

Provide information.

Examples:

* Market data platforms
* Research platforms
* Analytics systems

---

### Era 3 — Decision Systems

Purpose:

Improve decisions.

Examples:

* AI copilots
* Research intelligence platforms
* Trading intelligence systems
* Enterprise AI assistants
* Agentic workflow systems

The next decade will be defined by organizations that successfully build AI-native decision systems.

# Decision Intelligence Framework

A recurring theme throughout this repository is Decision Intelligence.

I define Decision Intelligence as:

```text
Data
    ↓
Context
    ↓
Intelligence
    ↓
Decision
    ↓
Action
    ↓
Outcome
```

The most valuable AI systems improve one or more stages of this process.
