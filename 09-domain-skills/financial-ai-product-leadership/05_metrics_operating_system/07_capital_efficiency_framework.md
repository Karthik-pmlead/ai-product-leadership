# Capital Efficiency Framework

## Measuring ROI of Intelligence Systems

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Capital efficiency measures:

> How much business value is generated per unit of intelligence cost

This is critical for evaluating AI at scale.

---

# 2. Core Formula

```text id="c1"
Capital Efficiency =
(Value Generated by AI Systems) / (Compute + Engineering + Data Cost)
```

---

# 3. Key Components

---

## 3.1 Intelligence Cost

Includes:

* Model inference cost
* Infrastructure cost
* Engineering maintenance

---

## 3.2 Value Generated

Includes:

* Revenue uplift
* Cost savings
* Risk reduction

---

# 4. Efficiency Metrics

---

## 4.1 Cost per Decision

Cost to generate one AI-assisted decision

---

## 4.2 Value per Workflow

Business value per automated workflow

---

## 4.3 Marginal Intelligence ROI

Return from incremental model/system improvements

---

## 4.4 Compute Efficiency Ratio

Performance gain per compute unit

---

# 5. Strategic Insights

---

## 5.1 Diminishing Returns Curve

More compute does not always equal proportional value increase.

---

## 5.2 Optimization Priority

* Reduce latency cost
* Increase decision density
* Improve reuse of intelligence

---

# 6. Enterprise Impact

* Better allocation of AI investment
* Improved platform scalability
* Clear ROI justification for AI systems

---

# 7. Strategic Principle

> Intelligence is only valuable when it is capital-efficient.
