# Decision Intelligence Framework

## Measuring the Quality of AI-Augmented Decisions

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Decision Intelligence metrics measure not just outputs, but:

> How effectively AI improves human and machine decision-making.

This shifts evaluation from:

* Model accuracy
  to
* Decision quality + decision velocity + decision impact

---

# 2. Core Concept

A decision is defined as:

```text id="d1"
Context → Options → Selection → Execution → Outcome
```

AI can influence every stage.

---

# 3. Decision Quality Metrics

---

## 3.1 Decision Accuracy

Was the chosen action optimal?

---

## 3.2 Decision Consistency

Are similar inputs producing stable outputs?

---

## 3.3 Decision Completeness

Did the system consider all relevant factors?

---

# 4. Decision Velocity Metrics

---

## 4.1 Time to Decision

Reduction in time from:

* Query → Decision

---

## 4.2 Time to Execution

Speed from:

* Decision → Action

---

# 5. Decision Impact Metrics

---

## 5.1 Outcome Uplift

Improvement vs baseline human decision-making

---

## 5.2 Risk Reduction

Decrease in adverse outcomes

---

## 5.3 Value Capture Rate

% of optimal value realized

---

# 6. Human vs AI Contribution

---

## 6.1 AI Influence Score

How much AI contributed to final decision

---

## 6.2 Human Override Rate

Frequency of human correction

---

# 7. Strategic Principle

> AI systems should not just automate decisions—they should improve them.
