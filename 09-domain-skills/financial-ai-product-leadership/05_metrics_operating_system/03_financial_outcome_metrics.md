---

# 2. Cost Reduction

# 3. Risk Reduction

* Loss avoidance
* Compliance breach reduction
* Error rate reduction

# 4. Capital Efficiency Impact

* Improved allocation efficiency
* Reduced idle capital

> AI value is only real when it appears in financial outcomes.
