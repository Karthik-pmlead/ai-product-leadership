# Enterprise AI Assistant

## Metrics Framework

---

# Executive Summary

Enterprise AI success is not measured by model performance alone.

It is measured by:

* Productivity improvement
* Workflow acceleration
* Knowledge accessibility
* Operational efficiency
* Risk reduction

---

# Metrics Hierarchy

```text id="mf1"
Model Quality
      ↓
System Adoption
      ↓
Workflow Efficiency
      ↓
Business Impact
```

---

# Layer 1

## Model Quality Metrics

---

### Retrieval Accuracy

Correct documents retrieved.

Target: >90%

---

### Answer Relevance

Quality of responses.

---

### Hallucination Rate

Must be minimized.

Target: <2%

---

### Tool Execution Accuracy

Correct system actions triggered.

---

# Layer 2

## Adoption Metrics

---

### Daily Active Users

Enterprise-wide adoption.

---

### Queries per User

Engagement depth.

---

### Workflow Integration Rate

% of workflows using AI.

---

### Repeat Usage Rate

Stickiness of assistant.

---

# Layer 3

## Workflow Efficiency Metrics

---

### Task Completion Time

Reduction in time per task.

Target: 30–60% improvement

---

### Search Time Reduction

Time spent finding information.

Target: >50% reduction

---

### Automation Rate

% of tasks fully automated.

---

### Context Switching Reduction

Fewer system hops.

---

# Layer 4

## Business Impact Metrics

---

### Operational Cost Reduction

Savings from automation.

---

### Productivity Gain per Employee

Time freed for high-value work.

---

### Process Cycle Time Reduction

Faster enterprise workflows.

---

### Error Rate Reduction

Fewer manual mistakes.

---

# Layer 5

## Risk & Compliance Metrics

---

### Policy Violation Rate

Target: near zero

---

### Unauthorized Access Attempts

Tracked and minimized.

---

### Audit Coverage

100% action traceability

---

### Compliance Override Frequency

Monitored for governance issues

---

# North Star Metric

## Enterprise Intelligence Index (EII)

```text id="ns1"
EII =

```
