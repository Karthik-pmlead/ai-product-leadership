# Goldman Enterprise AI Assistant

## Building the AI Operating System for Goldman Sachs

---

# Executive Summary

Large financial institutions possess enormous intellectual capital.

At Goldman Sachs, knowledge exists across:

* Research reports
* Trading insights
* Investment banking materials
* Risk analyses
* Internal policies
* Client interactions
* Historical decisions

The challenge is not information availability.

The challenge is information accessibility.

Employees spend significant time searching for knowledge, navigating systems, understanding policies, and coordinating workflows.

The next generation of enterprise productivity will be driven by AI systems capable of transforming organizational knowledge into actionable intelligence.

This document proposes a Goldman Enterprise AI Assistant that serves as the intelligence layer across the entire organization.

---

# Vision

Create a secure AI Operating System that amplifies the productivity and decision-making capability of every Goldman employee.

The assistant continuously helps employees:

* Find information
* Understand context
* Complete workflows
* Generate documents
* Navigate policies
* Access institutional expertise

while maintaining security, compliance, and human accountability.

# Strategic Opportunity

Large organizations face four structural inefficiencies.

---

## Knowledge Fragmentation

Information exists across:

* Wikis
* SharePoint repositories
* Research systems
* Email
* Internal applications

Employees often do not know where information resides.

---

## Institutional Memory Loss

Knowledge leaves when:

* Employees change teams
* Employees leave the company
* Projects conclude

Valuable organizational intelligence becomes inaccessible.

---

## Workflow Friction

Routine activities consume significant time:

* Searching
* Documentation
* Approvals
* Reporting
* Compliance reviews

---

## Decision Bottlenecks

Many decisions require:

* Multiple systems
* Multiple stakeholders
* Multiple approvals

This slows execution.
