# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Compliance Design

---

# Executive Summary

Wealth management operates within a highly regulated environment where advisors must balance personalization, fiduciary responsibility, and regulatory obligations.

The Wealth Advisor Copilot must be designed with governance and compliance embedded directly into product workflows.

The objective is not merely intelligent recommendations.

The objective is compliant and trustworthy advisory intelligence.

---

# Compliance Philosophy

The platform follows four principles.

---

## Human Accountability

AI recommends.

Advisors remain responsible for client advice.

---

## Explainability

Every recommendation must provide supporting evidence.

---

## Suitability First

Recommendations must align with client objectives and risk tolerance.

---

## Auditability

Every recommendation and action must be traceable.

---

# Regulatory Design Goals

The platform should support:

* Suitability obligations
* Fiduciary responsibilities
* Recordkeeping requirements
* Supervisory oversight
* Client transparency

# Client Suitability Controls

Purpose:

Ensure recommendations align with client circumstances.

---

Inputs Evaluated

* Risk tolerance
* Investment objectives
* Time horizon
* Liquidity needs
* Financial situation

---

Example

Recommendation:

Private Credit Allocation

Before recommendation delivery:

System validates:

* Accredited investor status
* Risk profile alignment
* Liquidity requirements

# Recommendation Governance

---

---

---

Evidence

---

Confidence
