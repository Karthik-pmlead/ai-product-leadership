# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Metrics Framework

---

# Executive Summary

Success is not measured by AI accuracy alone.

The platform must demonstrate measurable impact across:

* AI effectiveness
* Advisor productivity
* Client engagement
* Portfolio outcomes
* Business growth

---

# Metrics Philosophy

```text
AI Quality
      ↓
Advisor Adoption
      ↓
Workflow Efficiency
      ↓
Client Outcomes
      ↓
Business Outcomes
```

---

# Strategic Conclusion

The objective is not to deploy an AI assistant.

The objective is to help advisors deliver better advice at scale.

Every metric should ultimately answer one question:

Did the platform improve advisor effectiveness and client outcomes?
