# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## Advisor Workflows

---

# Executive Summary

The Wealth Advisor Copilot is designed to augment advisors across the entire client lifecycle.

The platform transforms fragmented client information into actionable recommendations that improve advisor productivity, personalization, and client outcomes.

This document outlines how AI supports key advisor workflows.

---

# Advisor Lifecycle

The platform supports five primary workflows:

1. Client Preparation
2. Client Discovery
3. Portfolio Advisory
4. Relationship Growth
5. Ongoing Client Monitoring

---

# Strategic Outcome

The Wealth Advisor Copilot does not replace advisors.

Instead, it shifts advisor time away from information gathering and administrative tasks toward client engagement, strategic planning, and relationship building.

The result is a more productive advisor, a more personalized client experience, and improved long-term wealth outcomes.
