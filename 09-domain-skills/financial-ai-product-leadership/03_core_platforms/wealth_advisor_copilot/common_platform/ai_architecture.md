# Wealth Advisor Copilot

## AI Architecture

---

# Executive Summary

The Wealth Advisor Copilot is an AI-native advisor intelligence platform designed to augment wealth advisors throughout the client lifecycle.

The architecture combines:

* Client Intelligence
* Portfolio Intelligence
* Recommendation Intelligence
* Generative AI
* Governance Controls

to create a scalable advisor decision-support system.

The objective is not automated advice.

The objective is trusted advisory intelligence.

---

# Design Principles

### Human-in-the-Loop

AI recommends.

Advisors decide.

---

### Explainability

Every recommendation must be traceable.

---

### Compliance by Design

Governance integrated into every workflow.

---

### Personalization at Scale

Support thousands of clients simultaneously.

# Strategic Outcome

The Wealth Advisor Copilot becomes the intelligence layer between client data and advisor decisions.

Its role is to help advisors deliver more personalized, proactive, and scalable advice while preserving trust, compliance, and human judgment.
