# Goldman Wealth Advisor Copilot

## AI-Powered Private Wealth Intelligence Platform

---

# Executive Summary

Wealth management is fundamentally a relationship business.

However, the modern financial advisor faces increasing complexity:

* Larger client books
* More investment products
* Greater regulatory requirements
* Rising expectations for personalization
* Continuous market volatility

The challenge is no longer access to information.

The challenge is delivering highly personalized advice at scale.

This document proposes a next-generation AI Wealth Advisor Copilot for Goldman Sachs that enables advisors to serve more clients, make better recommendations, and deepen relationships while maintaining human trust and accountability.

---

# Product Vision

Enable every Goldman advisor to operate with the support of a dedicated team of analysts, planners, researchers, and client service specialists.

The copilot continuously:

* Understands client goals
* Monitors portfolios
* Tracks market developments
* Identifies opportunities
* Generates recommendations
* Prepares advisor workflows

while keeping advisors in control.
