# Executive Summary

Financial markets are not simply collections of buyers and sellers.

They are complex adaptive systems composed of:

* Exchanges
* Market makers
* Institutional investors
* High-frequency traders
* Liquidity providers
* Algorithmic execution engines

Understanding market microstructure is essential for improving execution quality.

The purpose of Market Microstructure AI is to transform real-time market signals into actionable execution intelligence.

The platform continuously observes market behavior and predicts how execution decisions are likely to affect outcomes.

---

# What is Market Microstructure?

Market microstructure studies how trades occur and how prices are formed.

Key questions include:

* Where is liquidity located?
* How do orders affect prices?
* How does information move through markets?
* How do participants interact?

These questions directly influence execution performance.
