# Trading Intelligence Platform

## Execution Workflows

---

# Executive Summary

The primary objective of the Trading Intelligence Platform is to improve execution decision-making.

This document describes how AI augments workflows across the trading lifecycle.

---

# Workflow Categories

The platform supports:

1. Pre-Trade Intelligence
2. Execution Planning
3. Live Execution Monitoring
4. Post-Trade Analysis
5. Risk Surveillance

---

# Workflow 1

## Pre-Trade Intelligence

---

### User

Portfolio Manager

---

### Objective

Evaluate execution feasibility before submitting an order.

---

### Current Workflow

```text
Investment Decision
        ↓
Contact Trading Desk
        ↓
Review Market Data
        ↓
Estimate Liquidity
        ↓
Submit Order
```

---

### Future Workflow

PM enters:

"Evaluate purchase of 500,000 shares of NVIDIA."

Platform provides:

* Historical liquidity
* Expected market impact
* Volatility analysis
* Execution cost estimate
* Recommended timing

---

### AI Components

* Liquidity Model
* Impact Model
* Volatility Engine

---

### Success Metric

Reduction in execution uncertainty.

# Workflow 3

## Live Execution Monitoring

---

### User

Execution Trader

---

### Objective

Adapt execution dynamically.

---

### Current Workflow

Trader monitors:

* Fill rates
* Market conditions
* Execution performance

manually.

---

### Future Workflow

Platform continuously evaluates:

* Liquidity changes
* Volatility shifts
* Market impact

and generates recommendations.

Example:

```text
Market volatility increasing.

Reduce participation rate
from 15% to 10%.

Expected cost savings:
3 bps.
```

---

### AI Components

* Real-Time Market Intelligence
* Adaptive Recommendation Engine

---

### Success Metric

Execution quality improvement.
