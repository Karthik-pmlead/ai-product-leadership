# Trading Intelligence Platform

## Metrics Framework

---

# Executive Summary

The Trading Intelligence Platform exists to improve execution decisions.

Success cannot be measured solely through AI model accuracy.

The platform must demonstrate measurable impact across:

* AI performance
* Trader adoption
* Workflow efficiency
* Execution quality
* Business outcomes

This framework connects model intelligence to trading performance.

---

# Metrics Philosophy

The platform should be evaluated through five layers:

```text
AI Intelligence
      ↓
User Adoption
      ↓
Workflow Efficiency
      ↓
Execution Outcomes
      ↓
Business Impact
```

A highly accurate model that traders do not trust creates no value.

The ultimate objective is improved execution outcomes.

# North Star Metric

Execution Intelligence Index (EII)

Framework:

```text
Execution Quality
      ×
Decision Confidence
      ×
Execution Efficiency
```

The platform succeeds when it consistently improves execution decisions and trading outcomes.

---

# Strategic Conclusion

The objective is not to build an AI system.

The objective is to improve execution performance.

Every metric should ultimately answer one question:

Did the platform help traders achieve better outcomes?
