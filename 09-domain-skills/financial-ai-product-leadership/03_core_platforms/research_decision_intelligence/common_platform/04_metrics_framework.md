# Research Decision Intelligence Platform

## Metrics Framework

---

# Executive Summary

Traditional AI metrics such as accuracy, latency, and token usage are necessary but insufficient.

The Research Decision Intelligence Platform exists to improve investment decision-making.

Therefore, success measurement must extend beyond model performance and include:

* User adoption
* Workflow impact
* Decision quality
* Financial outcomes

This framework establishes a multi-layer measurement system connecting AI performance to business value.

---

# Measurement Philosophy

The platform should be evaluated using five layers:

```text
AI Quality
      ↓
Product Adoption
      ↓
Workflow Efficiency
      ↓
Decision Impact
      ↓
Business Outcomes
```

A model can be technically excellent and still fail as a product.

The objective is not to maximize AI performance.

The objective is to improve investment outcomes.

---

# Layer 1: AI Quality Metrics

Purpose:

Evaluate system intelligence.

---

## Retrieval Precision

Definition:

Percentage of retrieved documents that are relevant.

Formula:

Relevant Documents Retrieved ÷ Total Retrieved

Target:

> 90%

---

## Retrieval Recall

Definition:

Ability to retrieve all relevant information.

Target:

> 85%

---

## Citation Coverage

Definition:

Percentage of generated responses containing verifiable sources.

Target:

100%

---

## Hallucination Rate

Definition:

Percentage of responses containing unsupported claims.

Target:

<1%

---

## Financial Accuracy

Definition:

Percentage of financial statements interpreted correctly.

Target:

> 95%

---

## Latency

Definition:

Time to generate response.

Target:

<5 seconds

---

# Layer 2: Product Adoption Metrics

Purpose:

Measure platform engagement.

---

## Weekly Active Users

Definition:

Users interacting weekly.

---

## Monthly Active Users

Definition:

Users interacting monthly.

---

## Queries Per User

Definition:

Average platform interactions.

---

## Workflow Penetration

Definition:

Percentage of target workflows using platform.

Formula:

Platform-Assisted Workflows ÷ Total Workflows

Target:

> 60%

---

## Retention

Definition:

Percentage of users returning after 30 days.

Target:

> 70%

---

# Layer 3: Workflow Efficiency Metrics

Purpose:

Measure productivity improvements.

---

## Research Time Reduction

Definition:

Reduction in time spent gathering information.

Target:

50–70%

---

## Search Reduction

Definition:

Reduction in manual searching activities.

Target:

60%

---

## Brief Generation Time

Definition:

Time to create research briefs.

Target:

80% reduction

---

## Coverage Expansion

Definition:

Increase in companies or sectors covered per analyst.

Target:

2x increase

---

# Layer 4: Decision Impact Metrics

Purpose:

Measure decision quality.

---

## Time-To-Decision

Definition:

Elapsed time from research initiation to decision.

Target:

40% reduction

---

## Decision Confidence

Definition:

User-rated confidence in decision-making.

Target:

+25%

---

## Thesis Validation Rate

Definition:

Percentage of decisions supported by platform evidence.

Target:

> 75%

---

## Scenario Analysis Usage

Definition:

Percentage of investment decisions using scenario analysis.

Target:

> 50%

---

## Risk Detection Lead Time

Definition:

Time between risk identification and action.

Target:

+30% improvement

# North Star Metric

Decision Velocity Index (DVI)

Definition:

Measure of how effectively information is transformed into high-quality decisions.

Framework:

```text
Decision Velocity Index

=
Decision Quality
×
Decision Confidence
×
Decision Speed
```

The platform ultimately exists to maximize DVI.

---

# Strategic Conclusion

The platform succeeds when:

Analysts spend less time searching.

Portfolio managers spend more time allocating capital.

Risk teams identify problems earlier.

Organizations make higher-quality decisions faster.

Every metric in this framework should support that outcome.
