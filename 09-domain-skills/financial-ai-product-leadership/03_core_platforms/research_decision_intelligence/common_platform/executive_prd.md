# Research Decision Intelligence Platform

## Executive Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Document Owner:** Senior AI Product Manager
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Strategy Proposal
**Target Users:** Research Analysts, Portfolio Managers, Traders, Risk Managers
**Platform Type:** Financial AI Decision Intelligence Platform

---

# 1. Executive Summary

The Research Decision Intelligence Platform is an AI-native system designed to help financial professionals transform information into investment decisions more effectively.

The platform combines:

* Market data
* Research reports
* News
* Alternative datasets
* Enterprise knowledge

into a unified intelligence layer that supports research, portfolio management, trading, and risk workflows.

Unlike traditional market-data platforms that focus on information delivery, the platform focuses on decision support.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Financial professionals face three structural challenges:

## Information Overload

The volume of information continues to increase while human attention remains fixed.

Sources include:

* Earnings calls
* Regulatory filings
* Internal research
* Market news
* Alternative data

Critical insights are frequently missed.

---

## Workflow Fragmentation

Users operate across multiple systems:

* Research platforms
* Market terminals
* Portfolio systems
* Risk platforms
* Collaboration tools

Information does not flow seamlessly.

---

## Institutional Knowledge Loss

Organizations repeatedly lose context when:

* Employees leave
* Teams change
* Research becomes inaccessible

Historical investment reasoning is difficult to recover.

---

# 3. Product Vision

Create a Financial Decision Intelligence Platform that acts as an AI-powered partner for every investment professional.

The system should:

* Reduce information gathering effort
* Improve investment reasoning
* Accelerate decision-making
* Preserve institutional knowledge
* Support execution and risk workflows

# 17. Strategic Recommendation

Financial institutions are transitioning from information-centric workflows to decision-centric workflows.

The Research Decision Intelligence Platform represents the next generation of Financial AI infrastructure.

Its purpose is not to replace analysts or portfolio managers.

Its purpose is to increase the intelligence capacity of every investment professional and transform fragmented information into high-quality decisions at scale.
