# Research Decision Intelligence Platform

## AI Architecture

---

# Executive Summary

The Research Decision Intelligence Platform is designed as a multi-layer Financial AI system that transforms fragmented information into actionable investment intelligence.

The architecture must satisfy five requirements:

* High factual accuracy
* Financial domain awareness
* Explainability
* Enterprise governance
* Scalable decision support

The platform is not a chatbot.

It is a Decision Intelligence System.

---

# Architectural Principles

## Principle 1

Knowledge Before Generation

All responses should be grounded in verified sources.

---

## Principle 2

Decision Support Over Automation

The platform supports decisions.

Humans remain accountable.

---

## Principle 3

Explainability By Default

Every recommendation must provide supporting evidence.

---

## Principle 4

Enterprise Security First

Institutional knowledge is a strategic asset.
