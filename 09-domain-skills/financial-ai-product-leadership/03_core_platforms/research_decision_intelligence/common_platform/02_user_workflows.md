# Research Decision Intelligence Platform

## User Workflows

---

# Executive Summary

The Research Decision Intelligence Platform is designed to integrate directly into the daily workflows of financial professionals.

The goal is not to create another destination application.

The goal is to augment existing workflows and reduce friction throughout the investment decision lifecycle.

This document maps the current-state and future-state workflows for:

* Research Analysts
* Portfolio Managers
* Traders
* Risk Managers

---

# Investment Decision Lifecycle

Across most financial institutions, decisions follow a common sequence.

```text
Information Collection
        ↓
Research & Analysis
        ↓
Investment Thesis Creation
        ↓
Portfolio Decision
        ↓
Trade Execution
        ↓
Risk Monitoring
```

The platform is designed to improve every stage.

# Workflow 1: Research Analyst

## Current State Workflow

A research analyst evaluating a company typically performs:

```text
10-K Review
    ↓
Earnings Transcript Review
    ↓
Industry Research
    ↓
News Review
    ↓
Competitor Analysis
    ↓
Financial Model Update
    ↓
Investment Thesis Update
```

Time Required:

4–8 hours

Major Pain Points:

* Repetitive document review
* Information overload
* Manual synthesis
* Context switching
* Knowledge fragmentation

# Strategic Conclusion

The platform is not designed to replace financial professionals.

It is designed to increase the effective intelligence capacity of every participant in the investment process.

The ultimate objective is simple:

Transform information into decisions faster, more consistently, and with higher quality than traditional financial workflows.
