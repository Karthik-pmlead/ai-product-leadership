# Goldman Marquee Variant

## Reimagining Marquee as a Financial Decision Intelligence Platform

---

# Executive Summary

Marquee is one of the most strategically valuable AI opportunities within Goldman Sachs.

Today, Marquee primarily serves as a digital platform providing:

* Market intelligence
* Analytics
* Risk tools
* Pricing services
* Execution workflows

The next evolution of Marquee should move beyond information delivery and become a Decision Intelligence Platform.

Rather than helping users access data, Marquee should help users make better decisions.

---

# Strategic Recommendation

Marquee should not compete solely as a market data platform.

Market data is increasingly commoditized.

The long-term opportunity is to become the primary Decision Intelligence Platform used by institutional investors.

The institutions that win in Financial AI will not be those with the most information.

They will be those that help users transform information into decisions most effectively.

Marquee is uniquely positioned to become that platform.
