# Financial AI Governance

## Strategic Memo

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Governance is the defining constraint for AI adoption in financial services.

Without governance:

> AI cannot scale safely

---

# 2. Governance Pillars

---

## 2.1 Data Governance

* Access control
* Data classification

---

## 2.2 Model Governance

* Version control
* Evaluation gates

---

## 2.3 Agent Governance

* Action permissions
* Execution constraints

---

## 2.4 Workflow Governance

* Approval chains
* Risk-based escalation

---

# 3. Key Risks

* Hallucinated outputs
* Data leakage
* Unauthorized execution
* Regulatory breaches

---

# 4. Control Mechanisms

* Human-in-the-loop
* Audit logging
* Policy engines
* Real-time monitoring

---

# 5. Final Insight

> Governance is not a constraint on innovation; it is the foundation of scalable AI adoption.
