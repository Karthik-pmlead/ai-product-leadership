# AI in Capital Markets

## Strategic Memo

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Capital markets are becoming:

> machine-interpretable, AI-assisted, and partially autonomous ecosystems

---

# 2. Key Impact Areas

---

## 2.1 Trading

* AI-driven execution optimization
* Real-time decision systems

---

## 2.2 Research

* Automated synthesis of financial intelligence
* Multi-source reasoning systems

---

## 2.3 Risk

* Real-time risk detection
* Predictive exposure modeling

---

## 2.4 Portfolio Management

* AI-driven allocation systems
* Dynamic rebalancing models

---

# 3. Structural Transformation

Markets are shifting from:

* Human-driven interpretation
  to
* AI-augmented decision loops

---

# 4. Final Insight

> Capital markets will increasingly behave like real-time computational systems rather than human-interpreted systems.
