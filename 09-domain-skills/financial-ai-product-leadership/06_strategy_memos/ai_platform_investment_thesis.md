# AI Platform Investment Thesis

## Strategic Memo

---

# 1. Executive Summary

AI platforms in financial services represent a shift from:

> application-centric software

to

> intelligence infrastructure platforms

---

# 2. Value Migration

Value is moving from:

* Models → Systems
* Tools → Platforms
* Insights → Execution

---

# 3. Investment Opportunity

The highest-value layers are:

* Workflow orchestration
* Agent systems
* Decision intelligence layers
* Governance infrastructure

---

# 4. Competitive Advantage

Winning platforms will have:

* Deep enterprise integration
* Strong governance
* High decision velocity
* Domain-specific intelligence

---

# 5. Risk Factors

* Regulatory constraints
* Model reliability
* Adoption friction

---

# 6. Final Insight

> The future of financial AI is platform-driven, not model-driven.
