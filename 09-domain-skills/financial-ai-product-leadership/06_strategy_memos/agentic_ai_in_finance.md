# Agentic AI in Finance

## Strategic Memo

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Agentic AI introduces systems that:

> perceive → reason → plan → execute → learn

within financial workflows.

---

# 2. Core Capabilities

---

## 2.1 Autonomy in Workflows

Agents can:

* Execute multi-step processes
* Coordinate across systems
* Trigger actions with constraints

---

## 2.2 Multi-Agent Collaboration

Specialized agents:

* Risk agents
* Trading agents
* Compliance agents
* Research agents

---

## 2.3 Tool Integration

Agents interact directly with:

* APIs
* Trading systems
* Data platforms

---

# 3. Financial Use Cases

* Trade execution support
* Compliance validation
* Portfolio optimization
* Research automation

---

# 4. Key Constraint

> Autonomy must always be bounded by governance and compliance layers.

---

# 5. Final Insight

Agentic AI transforms finance from:

> decision-support systems

into

> execution-capable intelligence systems
