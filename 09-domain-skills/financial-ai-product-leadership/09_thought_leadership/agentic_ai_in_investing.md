# Agentic AI in Investing

Agentic AI introduces a shift from reactive analysis tools to proactive decision systems.

---

## Definition

Agentic financial AI systems are those that can:
- independently interpret signals
- coordinate multiple reasoning paths
- generate structured investment perspectives
- maintain context over time

---

## Multi-Agent Investment Model

A robust system typically includes:

- Market Intelligence Agent
- Macro Analysis Agent
- Portfolio Risk Agent
- Sector Specialist Agent

---

## Key Transformation

Traditional systems:
> User asks → system responds

Agentic systems:
> System continuously evaluates → synthesizes → proposes insights

---

## Strategic Value

Agentic AI enables:

- continuous research synthesis
- dynamic portfolio awareness
- multi-perspective reasoning
- faster hypothesis generation

---

## Risk Consideration

Without strong constraints, agentic systems risk:
- inconsistent reasoning
- overconfident synthesis
- lack of auditability

Hence, agentic AI must be paired with:
> structured reasoning + confidence calibration + compliance layers
