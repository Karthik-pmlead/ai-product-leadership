# AI in Capital Markets

AI in capital markets is fundamentally about improving how signals are converted into capital allocation decisions.

---

## Current State

Capital markets today suffer from:
- fragmented information flow
- high signal noise
- delayed interpretation cycles
- human-dependent synthesis

---

## AI Opportunity

AI systems can improve:

### 1. Signal Processing
- ingesting structured + unstructured financial data

### 2. Market Interpretation
- identifying patterns across macro and micro signals

### 3. Decision Support
- mapping insights to portfolio exposure

---

## Key Constraint

Unlike other domains, capital markets require:
- high precision
- low tolerance for hallucination
- explainable reasoning

---

## Strategic Insight

The most valuable AI systems will not predict markets.

They will:

> improve the quality and speed of capital allocation decisions under uncertainty
