# Human-in-the-Loop AI in Finance

In financial systems, AI does not replace human decision-making — it augments it.

---

## Core Principle

> Humans remain accountable for decisions. AI improves decision quality and speed.

---

## Why Humans Must Stay in the Loop

Financial decisions require:
- judgment under uncertainty
- regulatory accountability
- risk interpretation
- strategic context

These cannot be fully delegated to AI.

---

## AI Role

AI systems should act as:
- signal synthesizers
- reasoning assistants
- scenario generators
- risk evaluators

Not autonomous decision-makers.

---

## Optimal Interaction Model

Human + AI collaboration:

1. AI processes large-scale signals
2. AI generates structured perspectives
3. Human evaluates, adjusts, and decides
4. Feedback improves system learning

---

## Strategic Insight

The goal is not automation of decisions.

It is:

> amplification of human decision intelligence through structured AI systems
