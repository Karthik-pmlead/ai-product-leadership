# System Architecture

The platform continuously analyzes laboratory and production data to support operational decisions.

## Inputs

* Laboratory Systems (LIMS)
* Process Historians
* IoT Sensors
* Manufacturing Execution Systems
* ERP & Quality Systems

## Core Services

* Data Ingestion
* Feature Store
* AI Models
* Decision Engine
* Quality Intelligence
* Compliance & Audit

## Outputs

* Quality Predictions
* Process Recommendations
* Risk Alerts
* Executive Dashboards

The architecture enables explainable, AI-assisted decision-making across chemical operations.
