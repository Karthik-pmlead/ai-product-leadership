---

# 📈 Engagement Levels

| Level | Description | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| Unaware | No understanding of initiative | Awareness communication |
| Aware | Basic understanding but limited engagement | Share business context & goals |
| Neutral | Neither supportive nor resistant | Increase involvement & visibility |
| Supportive | Positive toward initiative | Reinforce ownership & advocacy |
| Leading | Actively driving change | Empower decision-making & sponsorship |

# ⚠️ Stakeholder Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |

| Communication gaps | Standardized reporting cadence |
