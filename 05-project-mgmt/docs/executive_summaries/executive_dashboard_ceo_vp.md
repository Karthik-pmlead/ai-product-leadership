# 📊 Executive Dashboard (CEO / VP View)

> A single-pane operating view of business outcomes, product execution, engineering health, AI systems, capacity, and organizational risk.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

This dashboard enables executive decision-making by consolidating:

- Business outcomes (growth, retention, efficiency)
- Product execution (roadmaps, OKRs)
- Engineering & platform health
- AI / MLOps system reliability
- Delivery predictability & capacity
- Risk, governance, and operational health

---

# 🧭 System Map

```text id="system-map-v2"

     ↓           ↓             ↓
Product Metrics   Capacity     Engineering + AI Systems
     ↓             ↓             ↓
        Governance + Risk + Execution Operations
```
