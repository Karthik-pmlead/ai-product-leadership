# 04 — Metrics and Experimentation

This section defines how AI products are measured, evaluated, and improved through experimentation, analytics, and metric-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

To ensure every AI product is:
- measurable
- testable
- optimized through experimentation
- aligned with business outcomes

---

# 🧠 Core Philosophy

If you cannot measure it, you cannot improve it.

AI systems require:
- product metrics (business impact)
- model metrics (accuracy/quality)
- system metrics (latency/reliability)
