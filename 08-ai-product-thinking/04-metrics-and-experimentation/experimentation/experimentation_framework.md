# Experimentation Framework

# 🎯 Objective

Design structured experiments to validate product and AI system changes.

---

# 🧠 Why Experimentation Matters

AI systems are probabilistic, not deterministic.

Therefore:
- assumptions must be tested
- models must be validated
- features must be measured
