# Business Context

Driver fatigue is a leading cause of road accidents globally. Existing solutions are either expensive hardware-based systems or non-real-time mobile apps.

This project builds a **real-time AI-based driver monitoring system** using:
- Computer vision (MediaPipe)
- Temporal signal modeling (blink + microsleep detection)
- Low-latency WebRTC streaming

## Problem Statement
Detect early signs of driver fatigue in real-time and trigger preventive alerts.

## Why It Matters
- Reduces accident risk
- Enables fleet safety monitoring
- Applicable in logistics, ride-hailing, autonomous safety systems
