# 01 - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Product Name

Financial Risk Graph Intelligence Platform

---

# Objective

Build a real-time graph intelligence system that:
- models financial entity relationships
- propagates risk dynamically
- detects anomalies
- provides explainable alerts

---

# Users

## Primary Users
- Risk analysts
- Market surveillance teams
- Fraud investigators
- Compliance teams

---

# Core Features

## 1. Interactive Risk Graph
Visualize connected entities.

---

## 2. Risk Propagation Engine
Propagate risk through connected nodes.

---

## 3. News Intelligence Simulation
Apply sentiment-driven risk updates.

---

## 4. Anomaly Detection
Detect highly connected high-risk entities.

---

## 5. Explainability Layer
Explain why alerts were triggered.

---

## 6. Real-Time Updates
Push updates using WebSockets.

---

# Success Criteria

- Real-time graph updates
- Dynamic risk score changes
- Explainable alerts
- Functional anomaly detection
- Smooth UI responsiveness
