Company: Tesla

Overview:
Tesla is a global electric vehicle and clean energy company operating across automotive, energy storage, solar energy, and autonomous driving technologies.

Core Businesses:
- Electric Vehicles
- Energy Storage
- Solar Energy
- Autonomous Driving Software

Strategic Strengths:
- Strong global brand recognition
- Vertical integration across battery and vehicle manufacturing
- Leadership in EV software and charging infrastructure
- Growing energy storage business

Key Risks:
- Margin pressure due to aggressive pricing
- Increasing competition from BYD and traditional automakers
- Regulatory and trade policy uncertainty
- Supply chain dependence on battery materials

Growth Opportunities:
- Expansion of energy storage deployments
- Robotaxi and autonomous driving initiatives
- Emerging market EV adoption
- Manufacturing localization

Recent Market Themes:
- Focus on Full Self Driving development
- Expansion of Gigafactory network
- Growth in utility-scale battery deployments
