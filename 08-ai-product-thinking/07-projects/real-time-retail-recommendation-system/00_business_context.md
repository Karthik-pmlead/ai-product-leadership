# 00 - Business Context

## Problem Statement

Modern e-commerce platforms struggle to deliver real-time personalized recommendations that adapt instantly to user behavior such as clicks, views, cart additions, and purchases.

Static recommendation systems fail to capture:
- real-time intent shifts
- session-level behavior
- short-term vs long-term preferences

---

## Business Opportunity

A real-time recommendation system improves:
- User engagement (CTR increase)
- Conversion rate optimization
- Average order value (AOV)
- Retention through personalization

---

## Target Users

- E-commerce platforms
- Marketplace apps
- Retail analytics teams
- Growth & product teams

---

## Core Value Proposition

Deliver **dynamic, behavior-driven recommendations** that update instantly based on user actions.

---

## Why This Matters

Companies like:
- Amazon
- Netflix
- YouTube

rely heavily on real-time ranking systems to drive revenue and engagement.

This project simulates a simplified version of such systems.

---

## Key Business Goals

- Increase engagement (CTR)
- Improve conversion rates
- Reduce user drop-off
- Personalize shopping experience
