# Business Context
## Industry Context

### Modern FinTech organizations face increasing risks from:

- payment fraud
- account takeover attacks
- money laundering
- bot activity
- compliance violations

### Fraud operations teams require systems capable of:

- real-time monitoring
- explainable decisioning
- scalable alert management
- operational intelligence
  
### Core Business Problem

- Traditional fraud systems generate excessive false positives while lacking transparency and operational efficiency.

### Product Vision

- Build an AI-assisted operational risk intelligence platform that improves fraud detection while enabling explainable human review.
