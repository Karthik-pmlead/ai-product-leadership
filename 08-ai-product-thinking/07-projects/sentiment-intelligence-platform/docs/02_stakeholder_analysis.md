# Stakeholder Analysis

# Overview

The platform impacts multiple business and technical stakeholders across operational intelligence workflows.

---

# Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities | Goals |
|---|---|---|
| Operations Analysts | Monitor operational issues | Faster issue escalation |
| Product Managers | Analyze customer sentiment | Improve customer experience |
| Trust & Safety Teams | Detect fraud/risk patterns | Reduce platform abuse |
| Executives | Monitor operational KPIs | Improve platform health |

---

# Technical Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| ML Engineers | NLP inference systems |
| Backend Engineers | Streaming APIs |
| Frontend Engineers | Operational dashboards |
| Data Engineers | Analytics pipelines |
| DevOps/MLOps Teams | Model monitoring |

---

# Stakeholder Pain Points

## Operations Analysts
- delayed issue detection
- fragmented review systems
- lack of explainability

---

## Product Teams
- limited sentiment visibility
- difficult trend monitoring
- regional blind spots

---

## Executives
- lack of centralized operational intelligence
- delayed customer issue awareness
