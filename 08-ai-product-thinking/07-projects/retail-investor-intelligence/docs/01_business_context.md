# Business Context

Retail investors today face fragmented financial data across multiple sources including price feeds, portfolio dashboards, and market indicators.

This fragmentation leads to:
- Delayed decision-making
- Cognitive overload
- Missed investment opportunities

## Problem Statement
There is no unified system that converts raw financial signals into actionable, prioritized investment insights in real time.

## Opportunity
Build a Decision Intelligence layer that:
- Aggregates financial data
- Detects meaningful signals
- Prioritizes insights
- Enables faster investment decisions

## Target Users
- Retail investors
- Active traders
- Portfolio managers (light usage)
