
# Business Context — Face Recognition Attendance System
---

# Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems such as RFID cards, passwords, fingerprints, or manual sign-ins often lead to:

- Buddy punching
- Attendance fraud
- Payroll disputes
- Long check-in queues
- Operational inefficiencies
- Poor auditability

Organizations require a secure, touchless, scalable, and automated attendance solution.

---

# Why AI?

Traditional rule-based systems cannot reliably verify identity in real time.

AI-powered face recognition enables:

- Real-time identity verification
- Touchless authentication
- Automated attendance marking
- Fraud prevention
- Operational scalability
- Better employee experience

---

# Why Face Recognition?

Compared to traditional systems:

| Method | Challenges |
|---|---|
| RFID Cards | Card sharing |
| Passwords | Credential leakage |
| Fingerprints | Hygiene/contact concerns |
| Manual Attendance | Human errors |
| Face Recognition | Touchless + automated |
