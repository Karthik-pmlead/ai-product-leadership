# Stakeholder Analysis — Face Recognition Attendance System

---

# Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Goals | Concerns |
|---|---|---|
| Employees | Fast and frictionless attendance | Privacy concerns |
| HR Teams | Accurate attendance tracking | Payroll disputes |
| Security Teams | Identity verification | Spoofing risks |
| IT Teams | Scalability and uptime | Maintenance complexity |
| Compliance Teams | Regulatory compliance | Data governance |
| Leadership | ROI and operational efficiency | Adoption and legal risks |

---

# Employee Perspective

## Expectations
- Fast check-in
- Contactless experience
- Minimal friction
- Transparent usage policies

## Concerns
- Facial data misuse
- Surveillance concerns
- Recognition bias
- False rejections

---

# HR Perspective

## Expectations
- Automated attendance tracking
- Reduced manual work
- Better auditability
- Payroll accuracy

## Concerns
- Attendance exceptions
- Employee complaints
- Operational reliability

---

# Security Team Perspective

## Expectations
- Strong identity verification
- Reduced unauthorized access
- Reliable authentication

## Concerns
- Photo spoofing
- Replay attacks
- Deepfake attacks

---

# IT & Infrastructure Perspective

## Expectations
- High availability
- Scalable infrastructure
- Easy integrations
- Operational monitoring

## Concerns
- Peak-hour latency
- Hardware failures
- Network dependencies

---

# Compliance Perspective

## Expectations
- Privacy compliance
- Secure biometric handling
- Consent management
- Data retention governance

## Concerns
- Regulatory violations
- Unauthorized access
- Data retention non-compliance
