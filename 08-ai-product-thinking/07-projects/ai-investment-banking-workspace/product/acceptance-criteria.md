# Acceptance Criteria

---

## Enterprise Search

Given

A logged-in banker

When

They search for a company

Then

Relevant results appear within two seconds with source citations.

---

## AI Copilot

Given

A user asks

"Summarize Microsoft's latest earnings."

Then

The response includes

- Summary
- Sources
- Confidence Score
- Links to original documents

---

## Meeting Brief

Given

Tomorrow's meeting exists

When

User opens Outlook

Then

Meeting brief is automatically available.

---

## Relationship Graph

Given

A company profile

When

Relationship Graph is opened

Then

Executives, board members, sponsors, and previous interactions are displayed.

---

## Deal Workspace

Given

Multiple active deals

Then

Users can filter

- Stage
- Owner
- Industry
- Region
- Risk
