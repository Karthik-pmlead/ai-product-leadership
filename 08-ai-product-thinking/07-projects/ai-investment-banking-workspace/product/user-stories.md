# User Stories

---

## Epic — Enterprise Search

### Story 1

As an Associate

I want to search for a company using natural language

So that I can quickly understand its financial position.

---

### Story 2

As a Managing Director

I want AI to summarize recent market activity

So that I can prepare for client meetings.

---

## Epic — Deal Workspace

As a Director

I want to see all active deals

So that I can identify execution risks.

---

## Epic — Relationship Intelligence

As a Coverage Banker

I want to view executive relationships

So that I know who should engage the client.

---

## Epic — AI Copilot

As an Analyst

I want AI to summarize a 100-page filing

So that I can focus on key insights.

---

## Epic — Meeting Assistant

As a VP

I want AI to prepare meeting briefs

So that I spend less time collecting information.

---

## Epic — Research

As a Research Analyst

I want semantic search across historical reports

So that I avoid duplicate work.
