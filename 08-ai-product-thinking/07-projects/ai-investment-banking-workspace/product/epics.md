# Product Epics

# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

---

# Overview

Epics represent large business capabilities that deliver measurable customer and business value.

Each epic supports multiple user personas and contributes toward the platform vision of transforming fragmented financial workflows into an AI-powered decision environment.

---

# Epic 1 — Enterprise Search

## Goal

Provide a single entry point for discovering trusted financial information across structured and unstructured enterprise data.

### Business Value

- Faster research
- Reduced information silos
- Improved knowledge discovery

### Success Metrics

- Search Success Rate
- Query Latency
- DAU
- Search Adoption
