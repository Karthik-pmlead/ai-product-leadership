# Product Strategy

---

# Strategy Overview

The objective is to build an AI-native investment banking platform that increases banker productivity while improving client outcomes through intelligent workflows.

Rather than delivering isolated features, the strategy focuses on creating reusable platform capabilities.

---

# Strategic Objectives

- Increase banker productivity
- Improve client engagement
- Accelerate deal origination
- Reduce manual work
- Improve knowledge reuse
- Increase AI adoption
- Strengthen platform scalability

---

# Data Strategy

Integrate:

- Market data
- Company information
- CRM
- Research
- Financial statements
- News
- Regulatory filings
- Internal knowledge

Unified data enables consistent user experiences and AI outputs.

---

# AI Strategy

Adopt a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture with:

- Enterprise search
- Semantic retrieval
- Source citations
- Confidence scoring
- Human review
- Prompt orchestration
- Multi-agent workflows

AI must be secure, explainable, and compliant with enterprise governance.

# Strategy Summary

The strategy is to evolve from a collection of disconnected financial applications into a unified AI-powered platform where data, workflows, and enterprise knowledge converge to help investment bankers make faster, more informed, and higher-quality decisions.
