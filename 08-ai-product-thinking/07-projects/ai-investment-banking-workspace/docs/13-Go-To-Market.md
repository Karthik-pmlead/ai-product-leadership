# Go-To-Market Strategy

---

# Launch Objective

Successfully introduce the AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace while driving rapid user adoption, measurable productivity gains, and sustainable organizational change.

---

# Target Customers

Primary

- Global Investment Banks
- Capital Markets Teams
- Advisory Businesses

Secondary

- Corporate Banking
- Equity Research
- Institutional Sales

---

# Product Positioning

An AI-native workspace that transforms fragmented financial information into trusted decision intelligence.

---

# Value Proposition

Instead of spending hours searching for information, bankers receive contextual intelligence, AI-assisted research, and workflow automation from a single workspace.

---

# Rollout Strategy

## Phase 1

Internal pilot

Users

50–100 bankers

Objectives

Validate workflows

Collect feedback

Measure productivity

---

## Phase 2

Regional rollout

Expand to selected business units.

---

## Phase 3

Enterprise deployment

Global rollout.
