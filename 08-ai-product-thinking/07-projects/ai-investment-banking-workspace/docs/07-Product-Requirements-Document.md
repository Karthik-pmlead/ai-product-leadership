# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

Version: 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

The AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace is an enterprise platform that combines market intelligence, relationship management, AI copilots, enterprise search, and workflow automation into a single product experience.

The objective is to reduce time spent searching for information while increasing decision quality throughout the investment banking lifecycle.

---

# Problem Statement

Investment bankers work across fragmented tools for CRM, research, market data, documents, spreadsheets, and communication.

The lack of integration creates:

- Duplicate research
- Slow pitch preparation
- Limited visibility into client relationships
- Manual workflows
- Knowledge silos
- Reduced productivity

# Functional Requirements

## FR-01 Enterprise Search

Users can search:

- Companies
- Deals
- Documents
- Research
- People
- News

Acceptance Criteria

- Results under 2 seconds
- Semantic search supported
- Source citations displayed

---

## FR-02 AI Copilot

Capabilities

- Ask natural language questions
- Summarize documents
- Generate meeting briefs
- Explain financial concepts
- Recommend opportunities

Acceptance Criteria

- Confidence scores
