# Product Vision

---

# Vision Statement

Create the industry's most intelligent investment banking workspace by combining enterprise data, AI, and modern product design into a unified platform that transforms information into actionable decisions.

---

# Mission

Empower every investment banker with trusted intelligence that accelerates research, strengthens client relationships, improves collaboration, and increases deal success.

---

# North Star

Reduce the time required for bankers to move from information discovery to informed decision-making.

---

# Why This Product Exists

Investment bankers spend too much time:

- Searching
- Researching
- Switching between systems
- Preparing documents
- Recreating knowledge

The platform exists to eliminate these inefficiencies while improving decision quality.

---

# Product Principles

## AI Augments Human Expertise

AI supports bankers rather than replacing them.

Humans remain accountable for every decision.

---

## Search Before Navigation

Users should ask questions naturally instead of navigating dozens of screens.

---

## Knowledge is an Enterprise Asset

Every report, meeting, transaction, and research artifact contributes to organizational intelligence.

---

## Workflow First

Technology should adapt to banking workflows rather than forcing users into rigid systems.

---

## Explainability by Default

Every recommendation should provide supporting evidence and source attribution.

---

## Platform Over Features

Build reusable platform capabilities that enable multiple banking workflows.
