# Business Case

# Executive Summary

Investment bankers spend significant time gathering information rather than advising clients.

The proposed platform increases productivity by reducing manual work, accelerating research, and improving opportunity discovery.

- Poor knowledge reuse

Business impact

- Higher operational costs
