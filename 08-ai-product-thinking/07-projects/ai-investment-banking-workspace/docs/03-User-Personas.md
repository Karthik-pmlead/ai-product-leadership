# Problem Space

---

# Background

Investment banking professionals interact with hundreds of data sources, documents, client relationships, and market events every day.

While financial institutions have invested heavily in data infrastructure, many workflows remain fragmented and manually intensive.

The challenge is no longer data availability—it is transforming data into timely, actionable decisions.

---

# Current Workflow Challenges

## Fragmented Information

Critical information is distributed across:

- CRM systems
- Market data platforms
- Research repositories
- Email
- Excel
- Document management systems
- Internal knowledge bases

Users spend significant time locating information before they can begin analysis.

---

## Manual Research

Research activities require:

- Searching multiple systems
- Validating data
- Comparing reports
- Reviewing filings
- Summarizing findings

This reduces time available for client engagement.

---

## Knowledge Silos

Insights often remain:

- Within individual teams
- Inside presentations
- Embedded in email
- Hidden within documents

Institutional knowledge is difficult to discover and reuse.

---

## Relationship Visibility

Bankers lack a unified view of:

- Executive relationships
- Historical engagements
- Previous transactions
- Internal expertise
- Coverage ownership

Relationship intelligence is often fragmented.

---

## Workflow Inefficiencies

Common pain points include:

- Repetitive data entry
- Duplicate research
- Manual pitch preparation
- Context switching
- Slow approvals
- Inconsistent reporting

---

# Product Opportunities

The platform should enable:

- Enterprise Search
- AI Copilot
- Relationship Intelligence
- Knowledge Graph
- Deal Pipeline
- Company Intelligence
- Document Intelligence
- Market Monitoring
- Workflow Automation
- Meeting Preparation

---

# Root Cause Analysis

The primary issues are not caused by insufficient data but by:

- Poor discoverability
- Lack of integration
- Limited automation
- Weak knowledge management
- Disconnected workflows
- Minimal AI assistance

---

# Design Principles

The future platform should be:

- AI-native
- Search-first
- API-first
- Workflow-centric
- Secure by design
- Explainable
- Human-in-the-loop
- Enterprise scalable

---

# Success Definition

A successful platform enables bankers to:

- Discover opportunities faster
- Reduce research effort
- Improve client engagement
- Increase collaboration
- Reuse institutional knowledge
- Make more informed decisions

Ultimately, the goal is to shift the user experience from searching for information to acting on intelligence.
