# User Personas

# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

---

# Overview

The Investment Banking Workspace serves multiple user groups across the deal lifecycle. While each persona has unique objectives and workflows, they all depend on timely access to trusted information, collaboration, and decision support.

This document defines the primary personas, their goals, challenges, and opportunities for AI-driven assistance.

---

# Persona 1 — Managing Director (MD)

## Role

Senior relationship owner responsible for originating business, managing strategic client relationships, and driving revenue.

## Primary Goals

- Win more mandates
- Strengthen executive relationships
- Identify new opportunities
- Increase revenue
- Reduce time spent preparing for meetings

## Daily Activities

- Client meetings
- Reviewing market activity
- Opportunity discussions
- Internal strategy reviews
- Executive presentations

## Pain Points

- Limited visibility across client activities
- Information spread across systems
- Time-consuming meeting preparation
- Difficulty identifying emerging opportunities

## Product Needs

- Executive dashboard
- AI opportunity recommendations
- Client relationship graph
- Meeting copilot
- Portfolio health view

## Success Metrics

- Mandates won
- Client engagement
- Pipeline growth
- Revenue generated

- Reduce manual research

## Daily Activities

- Company research
- Financial modelling
- Comparable company analysis
- Pitch book preparation
- Data validation

## Pain Points

- Copy-paste work

- Limited automation

## Product Needs

- AI research assistant
- Financial data search
- Comparable company finder
- Automated summaries
- Presentation generator

## Success Metrics

- Research time
- Analysis accuracy
- Document turnaround
- Productivity

# Shared User Needs

Across all personas, the platform should provide:

- Fast enterprise search
- Trusted financial data
- AI-assisted research
- Workflow automation
- Real-time collaboration
- Secure document access
- Personalized dashboards
- Explainable AI recommendations

---

# Jobs To Be Done

Users hire the platform to:

- Discover opportunities faster
- Prepare for meetings efficiently
- Find trusted information instantly
- Collaborate across teams
- Reduce repetitive work
- Make better decisions
- Improve client outcomes

---

# Design Principles

Every product decision should:

- Minimize cognitive load
- Surface relevant information
- Reduce context switching
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Keep humans in control
- Preserve trust and transparency

---

# Product Takeaway

Although user responsibilities vary, every persona ultimately seeks the same outcome:

**Spend less time searching for information and more time advising clients and executing high-value transactions.**
