# CRM Integrations

---

# Purpose

The CRM system is the system of record for client relationships and opportunity management.

The workspace enriches CRM data with market intelligence, research, and AI-generated insights to provide a comprehensive client view.

---

# Key Capabilities

- Client 360° dashboard
- Relationship history
- Opportunity pipeline
- Meeting summaries
- Contact intelligence
- Executive relationship graph
- Next-best-action recommendations

---

# Typical Integrations

- Salesforce
- Microsoft Dynamics
- Internal CRM platforms

---

# Synchronization

- Real-time updates for critical client events
- Scheduled synchronization for reference data
- Conflict resolution policies
- Audit logging

---

# Product Takeaway

Rather than replacing CRM, the workspace transforms it from a transactional record into an intelligent relationship platform.
