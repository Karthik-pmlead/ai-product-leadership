# Market Data Platforms

---

# Purpose

Market data is the foundation of investment banking workflows.

The workspace aggregates trusted financial information from multiple providers into a unified experience while preserving source attribution and governance.

---

# Supported Data Domains

- Equities
- Fixed Income
- Commodities
- FX
- Derivatives
- Indices
- Corporate Actions
- Financial Statements
- Estimates
- Ownership
- M&A Transactions
- News
- ESG Data

---

# Example Providers

- LSEG Data Platform
- Bloomberg
- FactSet
- S&P Capital IQ
- ICE
- Morningstar

---

# Integration Principles

- API-first
- Near real-time ingestion where appropriate
- Source provenance
- Schema normalization
- Data quality validation
- Versioned interfaces

---

# Product Capabilities

- Unified company profile
- Peer comparisons
- Market snapshots
- Historical trends
- Alerting
- AI-assisted summarization

---

# Product Takeaway

The value of the workspace is not owning market data—it is contextualizing trusted data within banker workflows and AI-assisted decision-making.
