# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

> **Reimagining the Investment Banking desktop with AI, Enterprise Search, Knowledge Graphs, and Intelligent Workflow Automation.**

---

## Overview

Investment bankers spend a significant portion of their day switching between research terminals, CRM systems, spreadsheets, emails, internal documents, market data platforms, and collaboration tools to execute deals. Valuable information is fragmented across systems, making it difficult to quickly discover insights, prepare client materials, track opportunities, and make informed decisions.

This repository presents an end-to-end **Product Strategy** and **Enterprise Architecture** case study for an **AI-native Investment Banking Workspace**—a unified platform that brings together enterprise search, AI copilots, financial data, workflow automation, and knowledge graphs into a single intelligent workspace.

Rather than replacing bankers, the platform augments them by reducing information friction, automating repetitive work, and accelerating high-value decision making across the investment banking lifecycle.

---

## Vision

Build the operating system for modern investment bankers.

Provide a unified AI-powered workspace that enables bankers to discover information, understand relationships, generate insights, collaborate efficiently, and execute deals faster through intelligent assistance.
