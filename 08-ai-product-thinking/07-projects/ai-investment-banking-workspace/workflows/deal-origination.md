# Deal Origination Workflow

# Investment Banking Workspace

---

# Overview

Deal origination is the process of identifying, qualifying, and developing potential investment banking opportunities before they become active transactions.

Successful origination depends on combining market intelligence, client relationships, financial analysis, industry expertise, and timely execution.

The AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace accelerates this process by surfacing opportunities, consolidating intelligence, and reducing manual research.

---

# Business Goal

Enable bankers to discover opportunities earlier, prioritize high-potential prospects, and improve mandate conversion.

# Current Workflow

1. Monitor industries and market activity.
2. Identify companies aligned with sector coverage.
3. Review earnings, filings, and strategic announcements.
4. Assess recent M&A, capital raises, or leadership changes.
5. Evaluate client relationships and prior engagements.
6. Build a target list.
7. Conduct internal strategy discussions.
8. Prepare outreach materials.
9. Engage the client.
10. Track opportunity progression.

---

# Pain Points
