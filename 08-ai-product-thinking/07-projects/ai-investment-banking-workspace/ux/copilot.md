# AI Copilot Experience

---

# Vision

The AI Copilot serves as an intelligent partner embedded throughout the workspace, providing contextual assistance rather than functioning as a separate application.

---

# Core Interactions

- Ask questions
- Summarize documents
- Prepare meetings
- Explain financial concepts
- Recommend next actions
- Generate research notes

---

# Context Awareness

The Copilot understands:

- Current company
- Active deal
- User role
- Recent searches
- Calendar events
- Permissions

---

# Example Workflow

User opens a company profile.

↓

Copilot automatically offers:

- Latest earnings summary
- Relationship overview
- Comparable companies
- Recent news
- Suggested discussion topics

---

# Trust Features

- Source citations
- Confidence indicators
- Feedback controls
- Regenerate response
- View supporting documents
