# AI Copilot

---

# Vision

Create an intelligent assistant that helps investment bankers complete research, prepare client meetings, generate insights, and navigate enterprise knowledge.

---

# Core Capabilities

- Natural language search
- Company summaries
- Financial explanations
- Meeting preparation
- Document summarization
- Deal recommendations
- Pitch book assistance
- Workflow guidance

---

# Example Prompts

- Summarize the latest earnings for Company X.
- Identify comparable M&A transactions.
- Prepare tomorrow's client meeting brief.
- Explain recent movements in the semiconductor sector.
- Find previous engagements with this client.

---
