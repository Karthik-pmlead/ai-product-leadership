# Agentic Workflows

---

# Purpose

Move beyond single-prompt interactions by orchestrating specialized AI agents that collaborate on complex banking workflows.

---

# Example Agents

Research Agent

Retrieves financial and market intelligence.

---

Document Agent

Summarizes filings, reports, and presentations.

---

Relationship Agent

Analyzes client history and internal expertise.

---

Market Intelligence Agent

Monitors news, earnings, and macro events.

---

Meeting Preparation Agent

Creates contextual meeting briefs and action items.

---

Orchestrator Agent

Coordinates multiple agents, resolves dependencies, and assembles the final output.

---

User Request
