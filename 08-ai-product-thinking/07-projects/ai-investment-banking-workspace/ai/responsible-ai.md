# Responsible AI

---

# Principles

- Fairness
- Transparency
- Accountability
- Privacy
- Security
- Explainability
- Human Oversight

---

# Financial Services Requirements

- Source attribution
- Audit trails
- Access controls
- Sensitive data handling
- Regulatory compliance
- Role-based permissions

---

# Governance

Every AI response should provide:

- Supporting sources
- Confidence indicators
- User feedback controls
- Escalation paths for uncertain answers

---

# Success Criteria

AI is trusted because users can understand, verify, and challenge its recommendations.
