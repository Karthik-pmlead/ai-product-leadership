# Hallucination Mitigation

---

# Objective

Reduce the likelihood of AI generating inaccurate or unsupported financial information.

---

# Common Causes

- Missing context
- Outdated knowledge
- Ambiguous prompts
- Poor retrieval quality
- Unsupported reasoning

---

# Mitigation Strategies

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Ground responses using trusted enterprise data.

---

## Source Citations

Require references for factual claims.

---

## Confidence Scores

Display confidence levels for generated responses.

---

## Permission-Aware Retrieval

Restrict context to data the user is authorized to access.

---

## Human Review

Require manual validation for high-impact recommendations.

---

## Continuous Monitoring

Track hallucination reports, user feedback, and retrieval failures.

---

# Success Metrics

- Hallucination Rate
- Citation Coverage
- User Trust Score
- Verified Response Rate

---

# Product Takeaway

In investment banking, trust is a prerequisite for AI adoption. Hallucination mitigation is therefore a core product capability, not an afterthought.
