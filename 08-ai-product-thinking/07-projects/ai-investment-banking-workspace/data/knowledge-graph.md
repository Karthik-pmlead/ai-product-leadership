# Enterprise Knowledge Graph

---

# Purpose

The Knowledge Graph connects structured financial data with unstructured enterprise knowledge to enable contextual reasoning, semantic search, and AI-assisted decision-making.

---

- Person
- Executive
- Client
- Deal
- Industry
- Security
- Financial Metric
- Research Report
- News
- Meeting
- Presentation
- Regulatory Filing

# Example Query

Show all healthcare companies that:

- Completed acquisitions in the last two years
- Have existing client relationships
- Recently appointed a new CFO
- Are covered by internal research
