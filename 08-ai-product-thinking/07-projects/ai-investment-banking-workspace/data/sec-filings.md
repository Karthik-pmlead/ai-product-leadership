# Regulatory Filings

---

# Purpose

Ingest and normalize public regulatory filings for AI search, financial analysis, and due diligence.

---

# Supported Documents

- Annual Reports
- Quarterly Reports
- Current Reports
- Proxy Statements
- Registration Statements
- Prospectuses

---

# Processing Pipeline

1. Document ingestion
2. Text extraction
3. Entity recognition
4. Financial metric extraction
5. Section tagging
6. Embedding generation
7. Indexing
8. Retrieval

---

# AI Capabilities

- Filing summaries
- Risk extraction
- Financial trend analysis
- Executive change detection
