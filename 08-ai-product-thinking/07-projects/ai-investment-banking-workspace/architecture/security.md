# Security Architecture

---

# Principles

- Zero Trust
- Least Privilege
- Defense in Depth
- Encryption Everywhere

---

# Identity

- Single Sign-On
- Multi-Factor Authentication
- RBAC
- Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC)

---

# Data Protection

- Encryption in transit
- Encryption at rest
- Key management
- Tokenization where appropriate

---

# Monitoring

- Security event logging
- Threat detection
- Audit trails
- Incident response

---

# Compliance

Support alignment with organizational policies and relevant financial regulations through access controls, logging, and governance.
