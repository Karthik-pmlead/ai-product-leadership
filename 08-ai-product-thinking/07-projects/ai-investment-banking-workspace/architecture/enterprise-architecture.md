# Enterprise Architecture

# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

---

# Executive Summary

The AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace is designed as a cloud-native, API-first platform that unifies financial data, enterprise knowledge, AI capabilities, and banker workflows into a single decision environment.

Rather than replacing existing systems, the platform orchestrates them through reusable services and shared capabilities.

---

# Business Objectives

The architecture supports five primary business goals:

- Accelerate banker productivity
- Improve decision quality
- Increase knowledge reuse
- Enable AI-driven workflows
- Scale globally with enterprise governance
