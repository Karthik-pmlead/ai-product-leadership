# Glossary

# AI-Powered Investment Banking Workspace

---

## AI Copilot

An AI-powered assistant embedded within the workspace that helps users discover information, summarize content, prepare meetings, and support decision-making.

---

## API Gateway

A centralized entry point for managing, securing, monitoring, and routing API traffic between clients and backend services.

---

## Deal Origination

The process of identifying, qualifying, and pursuing new investment banking opportunities before formal execution begins.

---

## DCM

Debt Capital Markets—advisory and execution services related to debt financing and issuance.

---

## ECM

Equity Capital Markets—advisory and execution services related to equity offerings such as IPOs and follow-on issues.

---

## Enterprise Search

A unified search capability that retrieves information across structured data, documents, research, emails, and knowledge repositories.

---

## Knowledge Graph

A graph-based representation of entities (companies, people, transactions, documents) and the relationships between them.

---

## LLM

Large Language Model used for reasoning, summarization, question answering, and natural language interactions.

---

## M&A

Mergers and Acquisitions—the advisory process supporting corporate acquisitions, divestitures, mergers, and strategic transactions.

---

## RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines enterprise retrieval with generative AI to produce grounded, explainable responses using trusted data sources.

---

## Relationship Intelligence

Capabilities that visualize and analyze connections among clients, executives, advisors, and internal teams to strengthen coverage and business development.

---

## Semantic Search

Search based on the meaning and context of a query rather than exact keyword matching.

---

## Workspace

The unified environment where investment bankers conduct research, manage relationships, collaborate on deals, and interact with AI-powered tools.
