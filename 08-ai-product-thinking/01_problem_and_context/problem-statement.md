# Problem Statement

## Business context
The business receives a large volume of unstructured customer feedback from surveys, reviews, support tickets, and social media comments.

## Problem
Teams cannot reliably understand what customers feel about specific aspects of the product or service, such as pricing, quality, delivery, support, usability, or reliability.

## Current pain
Today, feedback is reviewed manually or summarized at a high level. This makes it hard to identify the exact drivers of satisfaction and dissatisfaction, and it delays action on recurring issues.

## Business impact
Because the feedback is not broken down by aspect, product and operations teams miss early warning signals, customer pain points remain unresolved longer, and improvement decisions are based on incomplete insight.

## Desired outcome
We want an AI capability that can detect the aspect mentioned in a piece of feedback and determine the sentiment toward that aspect, so teams can act on specific problems faster.

## Example
Customer comment: "The product quality is great, but delivery was slow and support was unhelpful."
Expected output:
- Product quality -> positive
- Delivery -> negative
- Support -> negative
