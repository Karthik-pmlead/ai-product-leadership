# PRD Canvas

## Product summary
A chatbot that helps users ask questions, get answers, and complete tasks in a bounded domain.

## Product vision
Make conversational help fast, accurate, and safe enough to trust.

## Problem
Users need quick help, but manual support or search is slow, inconsistent, and hard to scale.

## Target users
- Customers.
- Employees.
- Support agents.
- Internal teams.

## Scope
- In scope: question answering, task guidance, escalation, retrieval.
- Out of scope: open-ended general intelligence.

## Core chatbot elements
- conversation flow.
- retrieval or knowledge grounding.
- fallback behavior.
- human escalation.
- policy guardrails.

## Success metrics
- answer usefulness.
- resolution rate.
- escalation rate.
- user satisfaction.
- time to answer.

## Assumptions
- A bounded domain exists.
- Knowledge sources are available.
- Human escalation is possible.

## Constraints
- Answers must be safe and grounded.
- Latency must be acceptable for chat.
- Sensitive information must be protected.

## Non-goals
- full open-domain chatbot behavior.
- replacing human experts in risky cases.
