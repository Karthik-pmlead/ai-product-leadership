# Governance and Ethics

## Policy goals
- protect user privacy.
- avoid unsafe outputs.
- make escalation clear.
- keep records for audit.
- require approvals for major changes.

## Review focus
- bias.
- hallucination.
- misuse.
- overreliance.
- privacy exposure.
- harmful or deceptive responses.

## Approval points
- use case scope.
- source content.
- launch readiness.
- policy exceptions.
- model or prompt changes.

## Auditability
Log user input, retrieved context, output, confidence, escalation decisions, and version information so changes can be traced later. Model governance in production also depends on traceable results, access control, testing, validation, and change logs. [web:240][web:117][web:115]

## Responsible AI principle
The chatbot should assist users, not replace judgment where accuracy, policy, or safety matters.
