# Bias and Fairness

## Purpose
Make sure the analysis is not misleading across segments or channels.

## Risks
- Overrepresentation of one channel.
- Underrepresentation of certain customer groups.
- Different language styles leading to uneven performance.
- Labeling bias in historical data.

## Mitigation
- Check coverage by channel and segment.
- Sample data from different groups.
- Review labels for consistency.
- Revisit taxonomy if certain aspects are missing or overused.

## Outcome
More reliable and balanced sentiment insight.
