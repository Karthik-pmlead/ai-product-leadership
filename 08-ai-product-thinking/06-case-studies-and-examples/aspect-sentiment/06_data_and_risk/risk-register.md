# Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Noisy feedback | Medium | High | Clean and sample the data before launch. |
| Bad taxonomy | High | Medium | Start with a small, business-approved taxonomy. |
| Privacy issue | High | Low | Mask sensitive content and restrict access. |
| Wrong sentiment label | Medium | Medium | Add human review and evaluation. |
| Aspect drift | Medium | Medium | Review and update taxonomy regularly. |

## Risk note
The biggest early risk is not model failure alone, but poor taxonomy design and low-quality data.
