# Build vs Buy

## Purpose
Decide whether to purchase an existing sentiment product or build a custom capability.

## Considerations

| Factor | Buy | Build |
|---|---|---|
| Speed | Faster to deploy | Slower initially |
| Custom taxonomy | Limited | Strong |
| Workflow fit | Generic | Tailored |
| Cost | Lower upfront | Higher upfront |
| Differentiation | Low | Higher |
| Control | Lower | Higher |

## Recommendation
For the first pilot, build a lightweight custom solution if the business needs a specific aspect taxonomy, custom dashboards, or integration with existing workflows. Buy only if the organization wants standard sentiment summaries with minimal customization.

## Decision note
Aspect sentiment is usually more valuable when it reflects the company’s own business categories rather than generic topic labels.
