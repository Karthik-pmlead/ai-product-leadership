# Monitoring and Alerting

## Purpose
Keep the system healthy after launch.

## Monitor
- feedback volume.
- confidence scores.
- aspect distribution.
- review queue size.
- error rate.
- drift in language or aspect usage.

## Alerts
- sudden drop in confidence.
- unusual spike in uncategorized comments.
- broken ingestion.
- review backlog growth.

## Goal
Catch issues early before business users lose trust.
