# 06 — Case Studies and Examples

This section contains end-to-end AI product case studies that demonstrate how real-world systems are designed, built, and evaluated.

Each case study follows a structured approach covering:
- Problem definition
- System design
- AI/ML components
- Metrics
- Challenges
- Outcomes

---

# 🎯 Purpose

To demonstrate applied AI product thinking across real-world systems including:
- recommendation systems
- AI copilots
- risk intelligence platforms
- decision intelligence systems

Unlike templates (which define how to think), case studies show how those frameworks are applied in real systems:

structured thinking → real-world execution

# 🏗 Structure of Each Case Study

Each case study follows a consistent structure:

## 1. Problem Statement
What business or user problem is being solved.

## 2. Objective
What the system aims to achieve.

## 3. System Overview
High-level architecture and components.

## 4. AI/ML Components
Models, algorithms, and intelligence layers used.

## 5. Workflow
End-to-end data and decision flow.

## 6. Metrics
How success is measured.

## 7. Key Challenges
Technical and product constraints.

## 8. Outcomes
Impact and results.
