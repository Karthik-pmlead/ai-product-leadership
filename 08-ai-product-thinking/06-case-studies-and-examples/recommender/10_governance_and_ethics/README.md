# AI Policy
Recommendations must be safe, explainable, and aligned to business rules.

---
# Approvals
Require approval for scope, launch, and major logic changes.

---
# Auditability
Log inputs, outputs, scores, and overrides.

---
# Compliance Checklist
- approved data use
- privacy controls
- fairness review
- logging and auditability

---
# Responsible AI Review
Check for bias, repetition, unsafe suggestions, and over-personalization.
