# AI PRD Canvas

## Product summary
A recommender system suggests the most relevant items to users based on behavior, context, or similarity.

## Product vision
Help users find the right item faster and help the business improve engagement and conversion.

## Problem
Users cannot easily discover the best next item on their own.

## Target users
- End users.
- Product managers.
- Growth teams.
- Content or operations teams.

## Scope
- In scope: candidate generation, ranking, re-ranking, feedback loop.
- Out of scope: full autonomous decision-making.

## Success metrics
- CTR.
- Conversion.
- Engagement.
- Retention.
- Coverage.

---
# Product Summary
This product recommends relevant items to users to improve discovery and business outcomes.

---
# Product Vision
Make discovery intelligent, relevant, and measurable.

---
# Problem
Users cannot efficiently find the most relevant item in a large catalog or content space.

---
# Target Users
| User | Need |
|---|---|
| End users | Relevant recommendations |
| Product teams | Better engagement and conversion |
| Business teams | Measurable lift |

---
# Scope
In scope: one recommendation surface, one user action, one item universe.
Out of scope: full personalization across all surfaces.

---
# Core Recommendation Elements
- User history.
- Item metadata.
- Context.
- Similarity.
- Ranking.
- Re-ranking.

---

# Success Metrics
- Click-through rate.
- Conversion rate.
- Engagement.
- Retention.
- Recommendation coverage.

---

# Assumptions
- Enough usage data exists.
- Items have metadata.
- Business can measure lift.

---
# Constraints
- Cold start for users and items.
- Latency requirements.
- Fairness and repetition concerns.

---
# Non-Goals
- Full omnichannel personalization.
- Perfect predictions.
- Autonomous decision making.
