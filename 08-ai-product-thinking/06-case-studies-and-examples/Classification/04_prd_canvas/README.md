# PRD Canvas

## Product summary
A sales operations triage system that classifies inbound items and routes them to the correct category, priority, or team.

## Product vision
Make sales intake faster, more consistent, and easier to manage at scale.

## Problem
Sales teams need to sort and route incoming items manually, which slows response times and creates inconsistency.

## Target users
- Sales operations teams.
- Sales development reps.
- Account teams.
- Revenue operations teams.
- Managers.

## Scope
- In scope: classification, prioritization, routing, escalation, review.
- Out of scope: autonomous deal decision-making.

## Core triage elements
- input item.
- predicted class.
- routing destination.
- confidence score.
- review status.

## Success metrics
- routing accuracy.
- time to route.
- review rate.
- handling time.
- downstream response speed.

## Assumptions
- A stable category set exists.
- Routing outcomes can be measured.
- Review support is available for low-confidence items.

## Constraints
- Items may be brief and ambiguous.
- Business routing rules may change.
- Data must be handled securely.

## Non-goals
- replacing sales judgment.
- end-to-end autonomous selling.
