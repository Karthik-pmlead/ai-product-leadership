# Governance and Ethics

## Policy goals
- protect customer and deal data.
- avoid incorrect automated routing.
- make review paths visible.
- keep records for audit.
- require approvals for taxonomy changes.

## Review focus
- bias.
- privacy.
- misrouting risk.
- overreliance.
- transparency.

## Approval points
- intake scope.
- routing taxonomy.
- launch readiness.
- exception rules.
- model or rule changes.

## Auditability
Log source item, predicted class, route taken, confidence, review result, and version history.

## Responsible AI principle
The system should help sales teams route work faster, not silently replace business judgment for important decisions.
