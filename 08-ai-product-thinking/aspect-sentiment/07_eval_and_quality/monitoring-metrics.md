# Monitoring Metrics

## Purpose
Track quality after launch.

## Metrics

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Aspect distribution | Whether one aspect is dominating unexpectedly. |
| Confidence trend | Whether the system is becoming less certain. |
| Review rate | How often human review is needed. |
| Drift signals | Whether feedback language or topics are changing. |
| Adoption | Whether teams are using the output regularly. |

## Monitoring note
Review metrics weekly during the pilot.
