# Red Team Cases

## Purpose
Test difficult or risky examples.

## Examples
- sarcasm.
- mixed sentiment.
- negation.
- multiple aspects in one sentence.
- slang or abbreviations.
- short one-word feedback.
- language switching.
- vague complaints.

## Goal
Find where the system fails before business users do.
