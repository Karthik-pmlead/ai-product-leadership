# Use Case Suitability

## Purpose
Determine whether aspect sentiment analysis is a strong use case to pursue now.

## Suitability scorecard

| Dimension | Score | Rationale |
|---|---:|---|
| Business value | 5/5 | Helps teams understand customer pain points quickly. |
| Data availability | 4/5 | Feedback is usually available across channels. |
| Model feasibility | 4/5 | Well-suited to text classification and extraction. |
| Time to value | 5/5 | Can show value in a narrow pilot quickly. |
| Operational readiness | 4/5 | Easy to place into dashboards or reviews. |
| Risk | 4/5 | Manageable if human review is used. |

## Conclusion
This is a strong early AI use case because it is measurable, practical, and easy to communicate to business stakeholders.

## Go / no-go
Go, provided the scope is limited to a manageable aspect taxonomy and one or two feedback channels in the pilot phase.
