# Product Vision

Build a reliable insight layer that helps business teams understand customer sentiment by aspect, so they can improve the right parts of the experience first.

## Vision statement
Make customer feedback understandable, actionable, and repeatable at scale.

## Long-term value
- Faster issue detection.
- Better prioritization.
- Stronger cross-functional alignment.
- More consistent customer experience decision-making.
