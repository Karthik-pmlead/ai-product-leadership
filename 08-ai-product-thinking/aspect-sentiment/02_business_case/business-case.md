# Business Case Summary

## Use case
Aspect sentiment analysis for customer feedback.

## Executive summary
The business receives large volumes of customer feedback across surveys, reviews, tickets, and comments. Aspect sentiment analysis can convert that unstructured text into structured insight about what customers feel toward key aspects such as product quality, delivery, support, pricing, and usability. This creates a faster path from feedback to action. [web:60][web:61][web:66]

## Why this matters
Today, teams know customers are speaking, but they do not always know which aspect is driving positive or negative sentiment. That slows prioritization and makes it harder to improve customer experience with confidence. A focused sentiment solution can support product, CX, support, and operations teams with repeatable insight. [web:55][web:65][web:67]

## Recommendation
Proceed with a narrow pilot focused on a limited aspect taxonomy and one or two high-value feedback channels. The pilot should be used to validate value, feasibility, and business adoption before scaling. [web:59][web:70][web:81]
