# Adoption Plan

## Purpose
Ensure the product is actually used.

## Plan
- place outputs where users already work.
- keep summaries simple.
- show trend and issue examples.
- add review rituals into team meetings.
- assign an owner for follow-up actions.

## Outcome
The system should become part of routine CX, product, or operations reviews.
