# Launch Plan

## Purpose
Describe the pilot launch.

## Launch steps
1. confirm scope.
2. prepare pilot dataset.
3. validate output on test cases.
4. train reviewers or pilot users.
5. launch to one team.
6. collect feedback.
7. iterate.

## Launch principle
Launch small, learn fast, then expand.
