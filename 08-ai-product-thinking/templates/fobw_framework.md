## Overview

The **FOBW Framework** is a structured approach for AI/ML projects that ensures systematic progression from problem identification to successful deployment.

- **F**ind: Identify the problem and opportunity
- **O**rder: Prioritize and scope the solution
- **B**uy: Build or acquire the capability
- **W**in: Deploy and realize value
