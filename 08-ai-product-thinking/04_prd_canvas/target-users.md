# Target Users

| User | Need | Value from the product |
|---|---|---|
| Product managers | Know which aspects need attention. | Better roadmap decisions. |
| CX teams | Summarize feedback across channels. | Stronger reporting and trend analysis. |
| Support managers | Find recurring pain points. | Better escalation and coaching. |
| Operations leaders | Detect process issues. | Faster operational fixes. |
| Executives | See customer experience health. | Clearer business oversight. |
