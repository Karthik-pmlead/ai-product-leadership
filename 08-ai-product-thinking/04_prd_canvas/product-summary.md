# Product Summary

Aspect sentiment analysis converts unstructured customer feedback into structured insight about specific business aspects such as quality, delivery, support, pricing, and usability. The goal is to help teams understand what customers like, dislike, and complain about most often. [web:56][web:66]

## Why it matters
Traditional sentiment analysis gives an overall positive, negative, or neutral label, but that is not enough when multiple issues appear in the same comment. Aspect-based sentiment analysis provides finer-grained insight that is better suited to customer experience and product improvement. [web:99]

## Outcome
Teams can move from raw feedback to prioritized action faster and with less manual effort.
