# Success Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| Aspect coverage | Percentage of comments assigned to one or more aspects. |
| Sentiment quality | Accuracy or usefulness of aspect sentiment output. |
| Actionability | Frequency with which teams use the insights. |
| Time to insight | How quickly teams can detect issues from new feedback. |
| Adoption | Whether the product becomes part of routine workflows. |
