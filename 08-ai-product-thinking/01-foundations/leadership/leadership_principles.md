# Leadership Principles

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define principles for leading teams, systems, and product execution.

---

# 🧠 Core Principles

- customer obsession
- ownership
- bias for action
- think long-term
- insist on high standards

---

# 🏗 Leadership Dimensions

| Dimension | Focus |
|---|---|
| People | Team alignment |
| Product | Vision clarity |
| Execution | Delivery discipline |
| Strategy | Long-term thinking |

---

# 🚀 AI Leadership

AI leadership requires:
- technical understanding
- ethical responsibility
- system thinking
- cross-functional alignment

---

# 🎯 Outcome

Builds strong leadership foundation for AI product environments.
