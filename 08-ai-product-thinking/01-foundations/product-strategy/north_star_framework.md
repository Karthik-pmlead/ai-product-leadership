# North Star Framework

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define the single most important metric that reflects product success.

---

# 🧠 What is a North Star Metric?

A North Star Metric represents:
- customer value delivered
- business impact
- long-term success signal

---

# 📊 Examples

| Product | North Star Metric |
|---|---|
| Recommendation System | CTR / Engagement |
| SaaS Platform | Active Users |
| AI Copilot | Task Completion Rate |
| E-commerce | Conversion Rate |

---

# 🔄 Framework Flow

```text
User Value → Engagement → Retention → Revenue
```

🚀 Outcome

- Ensures product alignment across teams and decisions.
