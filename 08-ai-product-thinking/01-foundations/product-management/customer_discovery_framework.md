# 📄 `customer_discovery_framework.md`

---

# 🎯 Objective

Understand customer pain points, workflows, and unmet needs before building products.

---

# 🧠 Discovery Principles

- talk to users early
- validate assumptions
- observe workflows
- prioritize pain points

---

# 🚀 Discovery Workflow

```text
Research → Interviews → Insights → Hypotheses → Validation
```

# 📊 Discovery Inputs
- Source	Example
- Interviews	Customer conversations
- Analytics	Usage behavior
- Support Tickets	Pain points
- Surveys	Sentiment signals

# 🧠 AI Product Discovery

AI products require understanding:

- trust expectations
- explainability needs
- workflow integration
- decision-support requirements

# 🎯 Key Outputs
- validated pain points
- customer personas
- workflow understanding
- feature hypotheses
