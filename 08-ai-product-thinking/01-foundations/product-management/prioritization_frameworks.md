# 🎯 Objective

Define methods for prioritizing product initiatives, features, and AI investments.

---

# 🧠 Prioritization Philosophy

Prioritization should balance:
- customer value
- business impact
- technical complexity
- strategic alignment

| Framework | Purpose |

# ⚙️ RICE Framework

```text
RICE = Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort
```

# 📊 Example RICE Table

| Initiative         | Reach  | Impact | Confidence | Effort | Score  |
| ------------------ | ------ | ------ | ---------- | ------ | ------ |
| AI Recommendations | High   | High   | Medium     | Medium | High   |
| Dashboard Redesign | Medium | Medium | High       | Medium | Medium |

🧠 AI-Specific Prioritization

AI initiatives should also consider:

- model risk
- explainability
- infrastructure cost
- evaluation complexity
- operational overhead

🚀 Strategic Prioritization Dimensions
| Dimension       | Question                           |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Customer Impact | Does it solve a major problem?     |
| Revenue Impact  | Does it improve business outcomes? |
| Technical Risk  | Is implementation feasible?        |
| Strategic Fit   | Does it align with vision?         |

🎯 Prioritization Best Practices
- avoid feature overload
- prioritize measurable outcomes
- optimize for learning velocity
- validate assumptions early
