# Stakeholder Management

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define frameworks for aligning stakeholders across product, engineering, leadership, operations, and business teams.

---

# 🧠 Stakeholder Philosophy

Successful AI products require:
- alignment
- transparency
- communication
- expectation management

---

# 🚀 Common Stakeholder Groups

| Stakeholder | Primary Concern |
|---|---|
| Executives | Business impact |
| Engineering | Technical feasibility |
| Design | User experience |
| Data Science | Model quality |
| Operations | Reliability |
| Customers | Usability and value |

---

# ⚙️ Stakeholder Alignment Framework

```text
Vision
  ↓
Goals
  ↓
Priorities
  ↓
Execution
  ↓
Metrics
```

# 📊 Communication Cadence
| Audience              | Frequency    |
| --------------------- | ------------ |
| Leadership            | Monthly      |
| Engineering           | Weekly       |
| Product Teams         | Daily/Weekly |
| External Stakeholders | Quarterly    |

# 🧠 Conflict Resolution Principles
- align on customer impact
- use data for decisions
- clarify tradeoffs
- escalate only when necessary
- document decisions

# 🚀 Executive Communication

Effective executive communication should:

- focus on outcomes
- quantify impact
- simplify complexity
- highlight risks
- propose recommendations

# 🎯 Stakeholder Success Metrics
- alignment quality
- delivery predictability
- reduced execution friction
- faster decision-making
