# Stakeholder Management

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define frameworks for aligning stakeholders across product, engineering, leadership, operations, and business teams.

---

# 🧠 Stakeholder Philosophy

Successful AI products require:
- alignment
- transparency
- communication
- expectation management

---

| Design | User experience |

| Operations | Reliability |
| Customers | Usability and value |
