# Product Operating Model

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define how product, engineering, design, analytics, and business teams collaborate to deliver AI-powered products effectively.

---

# 🧠 Product Operating Philosophy

The operating model emphasizes:
- customer-centricity
- cross-functional collaboration
- rapid experimentation
- measurable outcomes
- continuous iteration

---

# 🚀 Core Components

| Component | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Product Strategy | Defines long-term direction |
| Roadmapping | Prioritizes initiatives |
| Execution | Delivers customer value |
| Metrics | Measures success |
| Feedback Loops | Drives iteration |
