# Governance and Ethics

## Policy goals
- protect sensitive document data.
- avoid incorrect automated extraction.
- make review and correction visible.
- keep records for audit.
- require approvals for scope changes.

## Review focus
- privacy.
- document handling.
- accuracy risk.
- bias in document samples.
- overreliance on automated output.

## Approval points
- document type scope.
- field schema.
- launch readiness.
- high-risk document categories.
- model or schema changes.

## Auditability
Log source documents, extracted fields, confidence, review decisions, and version history so the extraction process can be traced later.

## Responsible AI principle
The system should assist document processing, not silently replace validation where the business impact of an error is high.
