# AI Risk Framework

---

# 🎯 Objective

Identify and manage risks in AI systems across the lifecycle.

---

# 🧠 Types of Risks

| Risk Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Data Risk | Poor or biased data |
| Model Risk | Incorrect predictions |
| System Risk | Infrastructure failures |
| Ethical Risk | Bias or unfair outcomes |
| Operational Risk | Production issues |

---

# ⚠️ Failure Modes

- hallucinations in LLMs
- incorrect ranking outputs
- drift in model performance
- data pipeline failures

---

# 🔍 Risk Detection

- anomaly detection
- monitoring dashboards
- drift detection systems
- feedback loops

---

# 🛡 Mitigation Strategies

- fallback models
- human-in-the-loop systems
- rollback mechanisms
- continuous retraining

---
