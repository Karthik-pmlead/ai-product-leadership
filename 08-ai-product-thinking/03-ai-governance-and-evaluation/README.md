# 03 — AI Governance and Evaluation

This section defines how AI systems are governed, evaluated, and made safe, reliable, and production-ready.

It ensures AI products are not only effective but also:
- responsible
- explainable
- compliant
- auditable

---

# 🎯 Purpose

To provide frameworks for:
- governing AI behavior in production
- evaluating model performance
- managing risks and failures
- ensuring responsible AI deployment

---

# 🧠 Core Philosophy

AI systems must balance:

- performance (accuracy, relevance)
- safety (bias, fairness, robustness)
- transparency (explainability)
- reliability (consistency, stability)

This section ensures AI systems are:
