# Responsible AI Framework

---

# 🎯 Objective

Ensure AI systems are ethical, fair, and unbiased.

---

# 🧠 Key Principles

- fairness
- transparency
- privacy
- accountability
- safety

---

# ⚖️ Fairness Considerations

AI systems must avoid:
- demographic bias
- training data bias
- ranking bias
- feedback loop amplification

---

# 🔍 Explainability

Every AI decision should support:
- "why this result?"
- "what influenced it?"
- "what alternatives exist?"

---

# 🔐 Privacy Protection

- data minimization
- anonymization
- secure storage
- controlled access

---

# 🚀 Responsible AI Checklist

- bias checked
- explainability enabled
- safety tests passed
- privacy compliance verified

---

# 🎯 Outcome

Ensures ethical deployment of AI systems at scale.
