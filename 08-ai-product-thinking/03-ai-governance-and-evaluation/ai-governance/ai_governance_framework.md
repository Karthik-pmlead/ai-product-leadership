```md
# AI Governance Framework
```

---

# 🎯 Objective

Define policies and structures for controlling AI behavior in production systems.

---

# 🧠 What is AI Governance?

AI governance ensures:
- controlled model usage
- compliance with policies
- monitored decision-making
- auditability of outputs

---

# 🏗 Governance Layers

| Layer | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Data Governance | Data quality and compliance |
| Model Governance | Model behavior control |
| Output Governance | Response safety |
| Operational Governance | Production monitoring |

---

# 🔐 Key Controls

- model approval workflows
- version control
- audit logging
- access restrictions

---

# 🚀 AI Governance Principles

- transparency
- accountability
- traceability
- reproducibility

---

# 🎯 Outcome

Ensures AI systems behave predictably and safely in production.
