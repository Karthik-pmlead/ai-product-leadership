# 06 — Data Strategy

# 🎯 Objective

Define how operational, customer, and business data is managed, processed, and utilized within the AI Decision Intelligence Platform.

---

# 🧠 Data Strategy Goals

The platform aims to:
- aggregate business signals
- correlate operational events
- support explainable AI workflows
- enable decision intelligence
- simulate enterprise operational analytics

---

# 🚀 Data Categories

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Customer Data | Reviews, sentiment signals |
| Operational Data | Incidents, latency metrics |
| Business Metrics | Conversion rates, sales |
| Trend Data | Regional performance |
| Workflow Data | Agent execution logs |

---

The MVP uses lightweight simulated datasets for:
- customer sentiment
