# Acceptance Criteria

## Purpose
Define what must be true before pilot approval.

## Criteria

| Area | Criteria |
|---|---|
| Aspect coverage | Most comments should map to one or more aspects. |
| Sentiment quality | Labels should be accurate enough for trend analysis. |
| Review handling | Low-confidence cases must be flagged. |
| Usability | Business users must be able to understand outputs. |
| Traceability | Every output must link back to its source input. |

## Go / no-go
Proceed only if the pilot demonstrates reliable aspect-level insight and acceptable business usability.
