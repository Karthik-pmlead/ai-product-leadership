# 11 - Tradeoffs

# Overview

The MVP prioritizes:
- simplicity
- explainability
- rapid prototyping
- architectural clarity

---

# Tradeoff 1 — In-Memory Graph

## Advantage
- fast iteration
- simple implementation

## Limitation
- no persistence
- limited scalability

---

# Tradeoff 2 — Rule-Based Propagation

## Advantage
- explainable
- deterministic

## Limitation
- simplified contagion modeling

---

# Tradeoff 3 — Simulated Data

## Advantage
- lightweight MVP
- fast development

## Limitation
- no real market integration

---

# Tradeoff 4 — Single-Service Architecture

## Advantage
- easy deployment
- low complexity

## Limitation
- limited horizontal scaling

---

# Future Improvements

- distributed graph systems
- event streaming
- temporal graph analysis
