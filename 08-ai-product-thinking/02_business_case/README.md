# 02_business_case

This folder captures the business case for the aspect sentiment use case.

## Purpose

The purpose of this folder is to justify why aspect sentiment analysis should be pursued, what value it creates, and whether it is feasible and worth prioritizing. It gives the business a clear view of the expected benefit before any solution work begins.

## Why this matters

An AI initiative should be driven by business value, not just technical interest. This folder helps the team decide whether the opportunity is strong enough to invest in. [web:30][web:130]

## Folder contents

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [business-case](business-case.md) | Executive summary of the use case and recommendation. |
| [roi-model](roi-model.md) | Quantifies the expected business benefit and cost. |
| [feasibility](feasibility.md) | Assesses data, technical, and operational feasibility. |
| [prioritization](prioritization.md) | Compares this use case against other AI opportunities. |

## Example use case

For aspect sentiment, the business case may show that customer feedback is too large to analyze manually, and that aspect-level insight can improve prioritization for product, support, and operations teams. [web:56][web:119]

## Output of this folder

By the end of this folder, the team should agree on:
- why this use case matters,
- what value it creates,
- whether it is feasible,
- and whether it should be prioritized.
