# Governance and Ethics

## Purpose
Ensure the aspect sentiment system is responsible, safe, and trustworthy.

## Governance principles
- Be transparent about what the system does.
- Use only approved data.
- Keep humans involved for uncertain or high-impact cases.
- Make outputs traceable and auditable.
- Avoid overstating confidence.

## Key concerns

| Concern | Why it matters | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Privacy | Feedback may contain personal or sensitive details. | Mask or restrict sensitive data. |
| Bias | Some channels or groups may be overrepresented. | Review coverage and label balance. |
| Transparency | Users need to understand the output. | Show aspect and confidence clearly. |
| Misuse | Teams may treat model output as absolute truth. | Add guidance and review policies. |
| Compliance | Data handling may be regulated. | Follow internal and legal requirements. |

## Review policy
- Human review for low-confidence outputs.
- Escalation for sensitive or customer-facing cases.
- Approval for taxonomy changes and major model changes.

## Ethical use
The system should support decision-making, not replace judgment in sensitive business situations.

## Deliverable
A governance approach that supports trustworthy and controlled use of the product.
