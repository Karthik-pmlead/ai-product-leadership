# Data and Risk

## Data sources
- help articles.
- FAQs.
- policy documents.
- support transcripts.
- approved knowledge base content.

## Data needs
- accurate and current documents.
- clear ownership of source content.
- versioned knowledge updates.
- access controls for sensitive content.

## Key risks
- hallucination.
- outdated answers.
- privacy leakage.
- biased or unsafe responses.
- poor knowledge retrieval.

## Mitigations
- use approved sources only.
- keep content fresh.
- log and review failures.
- restrict sensitive topics.
- add human escalation.
- use guardrails for risky outputs.

## Risk posture
The chatbot should start with a narrow domain and strong safety controls.

## Principle
If the knowledge source is weak, the chatbot will be weak too.
