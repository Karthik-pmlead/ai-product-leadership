# Business Case

## Opportunity
A chatbot can reduce support effort, improve self-service, and help users get answers faster.

## Business value
- Lower support load.
- Faster user resolution.
- Better customer or employee experience.
- More consistent responses.

## Feasibility
The use case is feasible if the business has a defined domain, a knowledge source, and a clear escalation path.

## Recommendation
Start with one high-value chatbot surface and measure whether it reduces friction or support demand.
