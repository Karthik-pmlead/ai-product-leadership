# Business Context

# Overview

Global e-commerce and marketplace platforms generate massive volumes of customer reviews daily across multiple languages and regions.

These reviews contain valuable operational signals related to:

- delivery failures
- damaged products
- counterfeit items
- customer dissatisfaction
- support escalation trends
- trust & safety risks

Traditional review analytics systems are often:
- reactive
- fragmented
- manually monitored
- difficult to scale globally

Organizations require AI-powered operational intelligence systems capable of:
- real-time review analysis
- multilingual understanding
- explainable AI insights
- operational alerting
- scalable analytics workflows

---

# Business Problem

Customer feedback is one of the most valuable operational datasets inside digital commerce platforms.

However, several challenges exist:

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|
| High review volume | Manual analysis becomes impossible |
| Multilingual content | Limited global visibility |
| Delayed issue detection | Slower operational response |
| Lack of explainability | Reduced analyst trust |
| Fragmented monitoring | Poor executive visibility |

Without intelligent automation:
- operational issues escalate unnoticed
- customer satisfaction declines
- fraud risks increase
- brand reputation suffers

---
