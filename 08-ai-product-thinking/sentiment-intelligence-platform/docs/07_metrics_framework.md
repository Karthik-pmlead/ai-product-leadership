# Metrics Framework

# Overview

The platform measures operational intelligence effectiveness, AI performance, streaming reliability, and customer experience trends.

The metrics framework supports:
- operational monitoring
- AI observability
- product analytics
- executive visibility

---

# Metrics Categories

The platform tracks:

1. Business Metrics
2. Operational Metrics
3. AI/ML Metrics
4. System Metrics
5. User Experience Metrics

---

# Business Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Negative Sentiment Volume | Measures dissatisfaction trends |
| Customer Experience Score | Overall sentiment health |
| Topic Escalation Rate | Operational issue growth |
| Fraud/Trust Signals | Risk indicators |
| Regional Complaint Distribution | Geographic operational visibility |
