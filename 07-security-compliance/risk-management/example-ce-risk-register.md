## How this ties into CE‑and‑product‑safety work

- **Product‑leaders** can align this register with **risk‑assessment** sections in the **Technical File** and use **Risk‑level** as input for test‑priority.  
- **Engineering teams** can treat mitigation‑columns as **hardware‑and‑firmware‑requirements** and evidence‑columns as **test‑case definitions**.  
- **Security‑/compliance‑leads** can point to this register in **CE‑Marking‑style reviews** to show structured, product‑safety‑driven risk‑thinking that includes **software‑as‑safety‑control** (e.g., watchdog‑timer, safe‑default‑state, recovery‑mechanisms).
